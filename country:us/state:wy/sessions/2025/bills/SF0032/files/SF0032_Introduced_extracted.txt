Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0256
SENATE FILE NO. SF0032
Unpaved roads speed limits-amendments.
Affairs Interim Committee
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to speed limits; authorizing local
2 authorities to set maximum speed limits on unpaved roadways
3 without first conducting a speed study or justifying the
4 maximum speed limit; requiring rulemaking; and providing
5 for effective dates.
6
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
8
9 Section 1. W.S. 31-5-303(b) and by creating a new
10 subsection (f) is amended to read:
11
12 31-5-303. Establishing maximum speed limits by local
13 authorities.
14
1 SF0032
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0256
1 (b) Except as provided in subsection (f) of this
2 section, in compliance with rules promulgated by the
3 department, local authorities in their respective
4 jurisdictions may determine the proper maximum speed for
5 all streets and highways within their respective corporate
6 jurisdiction which maximum speed is reasonable and safe and
7 which may be greater or less than the maximum speed
8 permitted under this act. The rules promulgated by the
9 department shall adopt standards consistent with national
10 practices. The department shall not promulgate rules that
11 require a speed study before a local authority establishes
12 a maximum speed limit for unpaved roads in accordance with
13 subsection (f) of this section.
14
15 (f) A local authority may establish a maximum speed
16 limit of not more than fifty-five (55) miles per hour for
17 unpaved roadways under W.S. 31-5-301(b)(iv) without first
18 conducting a speed study or providing justification for the
19 maximum speed limit.
20
21 Section 2. The department of transportation shall
22 promulgate any rules necessary to implement this act.
23
2 SF0032
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0256
1 Section 3.
2
3 (a) Except as provided in subsection (b) of this
4 section, this act is effective July 1, 2025.
5
6 (b) Sections 2 and 3 of this act are effective
7 immediately upon completion of all acts necessary for a
8 bill to become law as provided by Article 4, Section 8 of
9 the Wyoming Constitution.
10
11 (END)
