4 (a) The Wyoming veteran loan repayment program is
5 created to be administered by the University of Wyoming.
6
7 (b) To qualify for a loan under this section, the
8 applicant shall:
9
10 (i) Be a United States citizen or a permanent
11 resident alien who meets the definition of an eligible
12 noncitizen under federal Title IV requirements or
13 requirements of a subsequent similar federal enactment;
14
15 (ii) Be a veteran;
16
17 (iii) Be enrolled in good standing in a course
18 of study leading to a masters degree in social work or
19 counseling or a doctorate degree in psychology at the
20 University of Wyoming.
21
22 (c) Subject to the availability of funds appropriated
23 for this program, loans under this section may be granted

2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0083
1 to qualified applicants to pay the cost of attendance for
2 the academic programs listed in paragraph (b)(iii) of this
3 section. For purposes of this subsection, "cost of
4 attendance" means the sum cost of tuition, mandatory fees,
5 room and board, books and supplies, travel and personal
6 expenses to attend the University of Wyoming as determined
7 annually by the University of Wyoming.
8
9 (d) Any federal, state or other grants or funding,
10 other than loans received by the applicant for the cost of
11 attendance for a qualifying academic program shall be
12 prioritized by the University of Wyoming and applied before
13 a loan provided under this section. A loan provided under
14 this section shall not exceed the cost of attendance for a
15 qualifying academic program, reduced by the amounts of any
16 federal, state or other grants or funding received by the
17 applicant.
18
19 (e) A recipient of a loan under this section may
20 repay the loan without monetary payment by working not less
21 than twenty-five percent (25%) of his working hours
22 providing mental health services within this state to
23 veterans. To qualify for repayment under this subsection,

2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0083
1 work shall be performed within the minimum amount of time
2 necessary to repay the loan, plus three (3) years, which
3 shall begin with the calendar month following the month in
4 which the student completed the academic degree program.
5
6 (f) Any recipient of a loan under this section who
7 fails:
8
9 (i) To complete the academic program for which
10 the loan was provided shall commence monetary repayment of
11 the loan not later than forty-five (45) days after the
12 recipient leaves the academic program;
13
14 (ii) To obtain employment that provides mental
15 health services within this state to veterans within two
16 hundred forty (240) days after successfully completing the
17 academic program, shall commence monetary repayment of the
18 loan within two hundred eighty-five (285) days after
19 completion of the academic program.
20
21 (g) Loan repayment options under this section may be
22 deferred for a period not more than five (5) years while a
23 loan recipient is serving on full-time active duty in the

10

13
14 (iii) Criteria under which students may be
15 relieved from having to repay loans and interest thereon,
16 in whole or in part, where the requirement to repay would
17 cause undue hardship;
18
19 (iv) Criteria for determining the cost of
20 attendance as used in establishing the loan amount for the
21 academic programs listed in paragraph (b)(iii) of this
22 section, based upon each semester or summer school session
23 of full or part-time program attendance.
