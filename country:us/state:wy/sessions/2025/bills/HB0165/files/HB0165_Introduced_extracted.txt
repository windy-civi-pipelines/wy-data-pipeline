2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0478
Ranked choice voting-prohibition.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Webb, Brady, Bratten,
Guggenmos, Haroldson, Heiner, Johnson,
Ottman, Pendergraft, Smith, S, Webber and
Wharff and Senator(s) Hutchings and Ide
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to elections; clarifying that elections
2 shall not be conducted by ranked choice voting; clarifying
3 that ballots are required to specify only one (1) vote per
4 office to be filled; and providing for an effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 22-2-117(a) and 22-6-120(a)(xii) are
9 amended to read:
10
11 22-2-117. Vote required for election; ratification.
12
13 (a) Partisan and nonpartisan candidates who receive
14 the largest number of votes for each office to be filled at
1 HB0165
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0478
1 the general election are elected. Nothing in this election
2 code shall be deemed to authorize any election in Wyoming
3 to be conducted through ranked choice voting. Any existing
4 or future ordinance enacted or adopted by a county,
5 municipality or any other governmental entity that purports
6 to authorize ranked choice voting in violation of this
7 subsection is void. As used in this subsection, "ranked
8 choice voting" means a voting method that allows voters to
9 rank candidates for an office in order of preference and
10 has ballots cast to be tabulated in multiple rounds
11 following the elimination of a candidate until the
12 candidate or candidates with the most votes are declared
13 winners, or any other system that allows a voter to vote
14 for more than the number of candidates permitted to fill a
15 particular office.
16
17 22-6-120. Format of general election ballot.
18
19 (a) The general election ballot shall be printed in
20 substantial compliance with this format:
21
22 (xii) Adjacent to the description of any office
23 shall be printed "Vote for one" or if the office is to be
2 HB0165
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0478
1 filled by more than one (1) candidate shall be printed
2 "Vote For Not More Than", then the appropriate words and
3 figures designating the proper number to be elected;
4
5 Section 2. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
6
7 (END)
