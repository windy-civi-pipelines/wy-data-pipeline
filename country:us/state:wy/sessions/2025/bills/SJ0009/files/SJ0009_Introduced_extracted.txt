2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0768
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION NO. SJ0009
Sponsored by: Senator(s) Rothfuss and Case and
Representative(s) Brown, L, Provenza and Yin
A JOINT RESOLUTION
for
1 A JOINT RESOLUTION proposing to amend the Wyoming
2 Constitution to provide for a right of individual privacy.
3
4 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF WYOMING,
5 two-thirds of all the members of the two houses, voting
6 separately, concurring therein:
7
8 Section 1. The following proposal to amend Article 1 of
9 the Wyoming Constitution by creating a new Section 40 is
10 proposed for submission to the electors of the State of
11 Wyoming at the next general election for approval or rejection
12 to become valid as a part of the Constitution if ratified by
13 a majority of the electors at the election:
14
15 Article 1, Section 40. Right of Privacy.
1 SJ0009
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0768
1
2 The right of individual privacy is essential to the well-
3 being of a free society and shall not be infringed.
4
5 Section 2. That the Secretary of State shall endorse
6 the following statement on the proposed amendment:
7
8 The adoption of this amendment will provide that the right of
9 individual privacy is essential to the well-being of a free
10 society and shall not be infringed.
11
12 (END)
2 SJ0009
