Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0480
Domestic violence protection orders-affirmative defense.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Styvar, Brady, Filer, Singh
and Webb and Senator(s) Nethercott
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to domestic violence protection; providing
2 a conforming affirmative defense to a charge of a violation
3 of a domestic violence protection order; making conforming
4 amendments; and providing for an effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 35-21-105(g) and by creating a new
9 subsection (k) is amended to read:
10
11 35-21-105. Order of protection; contents; remedies;
12 order not to affect title to property; conditions.
13
14 (g) Except as provided in subsection (k) of this
15 section, no act of the petitioner or the respondent may be
1 HB0127
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0480
1 construed to waive or nullify any provision of an order of
2 protection.
3
4 (k) A request by the victim for the perpetrator to
5 violate an order of protection issued under this section or
6 a temporary order of protection issued under W.S. 35-21-104
7 shall constitute an affirmative defense to a charge of
8 violation of the order by the perpetrator.
9
10 Section 2. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
11
12 (END)
