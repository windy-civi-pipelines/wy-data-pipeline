7 "Section 2.

9 (a) Subject to subsection (c) of this section, the secretary
10 of state is authorized up to one (1) full-time position for the
11 period beginning with the effective date of this act and ending
12 June 30, 2026 for purposes of implementing this act. It is the
13 intent of the legislature that the secretary of state include this
14 full-time position in its standard budget request for the
15 immediately succeeding fiscal biennium.
16
17 (b) Subject to subsection (c) of this section, there is
18 appropriated ninety-three thousand one hundred seventy-nine
19 dollars ($93,179.00) from the general fund to the secretary of
20 state for the period beginning with the effective date of this act
21 and ending June 30, 2026 to be expended only for the purposes of
22 funding the position authorized in subsection (a) of this section.
23 This appropriation shall not be transferred or expended for any
24 other purpose and any unexpended, unobligated funds remaining from
25 this appropriation shall revert as provided by law on June 30,
26 2026. It is the intent of the legislature that this appropriation
27 be doubled and included in the standard budget request of the
28 secretary of state for the immediately succeeding fiscal biennium.
29
30 (c) The authorization and appropriation under subsections
31 (a) and (b) of this section shall not be effective if 2025 House
32 Bill 0001 or Senate File 0001, as enacted into law, authorizes at
33 least one (1) additional full-time position for the secretary of
34 state compared to the number of positions authorized in 2024
35 Wyoming Session Laws, Chapter 118, Section 002.".
