A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to mobile homes; removing a requirement for
2 the cancellation of certificates of title for mobile homes;
3 and providing for an effective date.
4
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
6
7 Section 1. W.S. 31-2-502(b)(i) is amended to read:
8
9 31-2-502. Exclusions.
10
11 (b) If a mobile home is installed on a permanent
12 foundation and is taxable as real property:
13
14 (i) The certificate of title or manufacturer's
15 certificate of origin, if any, shall be surrendered to and

1 cancelled by the county clerk of the county in which the
2 mobile home is located except that no title shall be
3 cancelled under this subsection unless all liens on the
4 home have been released. The county clerk may require the
5 person surrendering the title for cancellation to disclose
6 information necessary to determine whether cancellation is
7 proper under law. The county clerk shall issue a document
8 certifying the cancellation of the certificate of title for
9 recording in the real estate records of the county clerk of
10 the county in which the mobile home is located;
11
12 Section 2. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
