Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0361
Trespassing-suspend hunting license.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Allemand and Campbell, K
and Senator(s) French and Ide
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to game and fish; prohibiting hunters who
2 are convicted of trespassing on private land from
3 purchasing hunting, fishing and trapping licenses as
4 specified; requiring rulemaking; and providing for
5 effective dates.
6
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
8
9 Section 1. W.S. 23-3-305(b) is amended to read:
10
11 23-3-305. Hunting from highway; entering or traveling
12 through private property without permission; penalty;
13 hunting at night without permission prohibited.
14
1 HB0109
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0361
1 (b) No person shall enter upon, travel through or
2 return across the private property of any person to take
3 wildlife, hunt, fish, collect antlers or horns, or trap
4 without the permission of the owner or person in charge of
5 the property. Violation of this subsection constitutes a
6 low misdemeanor punishable as provided in W.S.
7 23-6-202(a)(v) shall result in the person losing hunting,
8 fishing and trapping license privileges for two (2) years
9 from the date of the violation in addition to the
10 penalties. For purposes of this subsection, "travel through
11 and or return across" requires physically touching or
12 driving on the surface of the private property.
13
14 Section 2. The game and fish commission shall
15 promulgate all rules necessary to implement this act.
16
2 HB0109
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0361
1 Section 3.
2
3 (a) Except as provided in subsection (b) of this
4 section, this act is effective July 1, 2025.
5
6 (b) Sections 2 and 3 of this act are effective
7 immediately upon completion of all acts necessary for a
8 bill to become law as provided by Article 4, Section 8 of
9 the Wyoming Constitution.
10
11 (END)
