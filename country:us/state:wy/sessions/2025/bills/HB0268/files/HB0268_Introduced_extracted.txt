Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0510
People's parking act.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Singh and Filer and
Senator(s) Hutchings
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to cities and towns; requiring a vote of
2 qualified electors to approve the use of paid parking
3 meters or zones by cities and towns; and providing for an
4 effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 15-1-103(a) by creating a new
9 paragraph (li) is amended to read:
10
11 15-1-103. General powers of governing bodies.
12
13 (a) The governing bodies of all cities and towns may:
14
1 HB0268
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0510
1 (li) Establish and maintain paid parking meters
2 or zones requiring the deposit therein of renumeration
3 either in cash or through a digital payment system for the
4 privilege of parking cars or other vehicles upon any
5 streets or highways in the city or town only upon a
6 majority vote of qualified electors at an election held in
7 the same manner as provided by law for the submission of a
8 bond issue under W.S. 22-21-101 through 22-21-112. Any
9 ballot proposition pursuant to this paragraph shall include
10 a description of the proposed location of the paid parking
11 meters or zones.
12
13 Section 2. This act shall apply to all existing,
14 planned and future paid parking meters or zones on and
15 after the effective date of this act.
16
17 Section 3. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
18
19 (END)
