2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0205
SENATE FILE NO. SF0059
Registered agents-release of records.
Subdivisions Interim Committee
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to registered offices and agents;
2 authorizing the secretary of state to release confidential
3 information maintained by registered agents as specified;
4 and providing for an effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 17-28-108(c) is amended to read:
9
10 17-28-108. Production of records.
11
12 (c) The secretary of state shall hold any records
13 obtained pursuant to this section confidential except for
14 information already on file with the secretary of state as
15 part of a public document and information required to be in
1 SF0059
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0205
1 the annual report required by W.S. 17-16-1630(a). The
2 secretary of state may release any such confidential
3 information only pursuant to court ordered subpoena, or to
4 a bona fide law enforcement agency for use in a criminal
5 investigation or, upon written application, to a county
6 assessor or county treasurer to obtain the information for
7 official business. Any records obtained by a county
8 assessor or county treasurer pursuant to this subsection
9 shall be held confidential, provided that the county
10 assessor or county treasurer may release such confidential
11 information only pursuant to a court ordered subpoena or to
12 a bona fide law enforcement agency for use in a criminal
13 investigation.
14
15 Section 2. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
