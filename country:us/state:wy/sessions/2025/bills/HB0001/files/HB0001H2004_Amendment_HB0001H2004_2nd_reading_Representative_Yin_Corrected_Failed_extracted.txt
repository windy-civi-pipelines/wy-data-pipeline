19 1. Of this general fund appropriation, ninety thousand dollars ($90,000.00) is
20 for costs of publication required by W.S. 22-20-104 for any joint resolution
21 adopted by the legislature that would propose amendment to the constitution on
22 the 2024 statewide election ballot. It is the intent of the legislature that
23 this appropriation not be included in the secretary of state's standard budget
24 for the immediately succeeding fiscal biennium. This appropriation shall not be
25 transferred or expended for any other purpose and any unexpended, unobligated
26 funds remaining from this appropriation shall revert as provided by law on
27 December 31, 2024.
28
29 2. Of this general fund appropriation, three million one hundred eighteen
30 thousand five hundred thirty dollars ($3,118,530.00) is appropriated for
31 purposes of upgrading or replacing the secretary of state's enterprise
32 application for business entity formations and amendments filed online and for
33 purposes of integrating notary registrations, apostille requests and lobbyist
34 registrations in the online application platform. Any contract to implement
35 this footnote shall be made by competitive sealed bidding as provided in W.S.
36 9-2-3204(b)(iv)(A). This appropriation shall not be transferred or expended for
37 any other purpose. It is the intent of the legislature that this appropriation
38 not be included in the secretary of state's standard budget for the immediately
39 succeeding fiscal biennium.".
