6 "[STATE CAPITOL COMPLEX HOTEL AND CONFERENCE CENTER]
7
8 Section 351.
9
10 (a) The state building commission shall conduct a
11 feasibility study regarding constructing a hotel and conference
12 center in the vicinity of the Wyoming state capitol building,
13 preferably near or within the state capitol complex, the revenue
14 of which shall be deposited into the school foundation program
15 account. The commission shall contact persons who are interested
16 in developing, constructing and operating the hotel and conference
17 center and shall solicit bids to determine the costs involved with
18 the project. The state building commission shall coordinate with
19 the state treasurer to determine the anticipated annual revenue
20 for the state that could result from the project.
21
22 (b) Not later than November 1, 2025, the state building
23 commission shall report to the joint appropriations committee and
24 the select committee on capital financing and investments
25 regarding the findings of the feasibility study required by
26 subsection (a) of this section.
27
28 (c) There is appropriated twenty-five thousand dollars
29 ($25,000.00) from the general fund to the state building commission
30 for purposes of conducting the feasibility study required by this
31 section. This appropriation shall not be transferred or expended
32 for any other purpose. It is the intent of the legislature that
33 this appropriation not be included in the state building commission
34 or state construction department's standard budget for the
35 immediately succeeding fiscal biennium.".
