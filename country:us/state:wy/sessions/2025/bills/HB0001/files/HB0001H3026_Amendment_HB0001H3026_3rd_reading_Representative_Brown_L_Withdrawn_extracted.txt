27 1. The department of education shall maintain the total amount of direct
28 expenditures for and grants to local school districts in support of applied
29 agriculture and farming programs during the 2025-2026 fiscal biennium as
30 compared to the 2023-2024 fiscal biennium. It is the intent of the legislature
31 that any expenditures by the department of education pursuant to this footnote
32 be documented and included in the department of education's standard budget for
33 the immediately succeeding fiscal biennium.
34
35 2. Of this general fund appropriation, two hundred sixty thousand dollars
36 ($260,000.00) is appropriated, and of these authorized employees, one (1) full-
37 time position is authorized for increased staffing in the finance unit.
38
39 3. Of this general fund appropriation, thirty-three thousand four hundred
40 seventy-six dollars ($33,476.00) is appropriated for increased compensation and
41 employer paid benefits for two (2) positions within the finance unit.
42
43 4. Of this general fund appropriation, one hundred thousand dollars
44 ($100,000.00) is appropriated for the advancement of civics education through
1
1 support of the Wyoming boys' state and Wyoming girls' state programs in the
2 amount of twenty-five thousand dollars ($25,000.00) annually to each program.
3 It is the intent of the legislature that this appropriation be included in the
4 department of education's standard budget for the immediately succeeding fiscal
5 biennium.
6
7 5. Of this general fund appropriation, fifty thousand dollars ($50,000.00) is
8 appropriated to pay for processing costs for Wyoming poultry, lamb, pork, beef
9 or bison donated to a school district to be used in school meals. Expenditures
10 authorized in this footnote shall be made only if an equal amount of funding
11 has been contributed by a local school district for the processing costs of the
12 donated Wyoming poultry, lamb, pork, beef or bison. The department shall
13 endeavor to provide funding to as many school districts as possible under this
14 footnote and participation in the federal school lunch program shall not be
15 used as a limiting criterion for the distribution of these funds. To the extent
16 all funds from this appropriation are expended, the department of education
17 shall identify other funds appropriated in this section for continuation of the
18 expenditures under this footnote.
19
20 6. Of this general fund appropriation, four hundred ninety-six thousand eight
21 dollars ($496,008.00) is appropriated, and of these authorized employees, two
22 (2) full-time positions are authorized for outreach for the deaf and hard of
23 hearing unit.
24
25 7. Of this general fund appropriation, one hundred fifty thousand dollars
26 ($150,000.00) is appropriated, and of the authorized employees one (1) full-
27 time employee position is authorized, for purposes of the department of
28 education's comprehensive state literacy plan. The department of education shall
29 endeavor to hire a person with experience in childhood literacy to fill the
30 full-time employee position authorized by this footnote. This appropriation
31 shall not be transferred or expended for any other purpose. It is the intent of
32 the legislature that this appropriation be doubled and included in the
33 department of education's standard budget for the immediately succeeding fiscal
34 biennium.".
35
36 To the extent required by this amendment: adjust totals; and
37 renumber as necessary; and remove any duplication of language or
38 tables. BROWN, L, HARSHMAN
