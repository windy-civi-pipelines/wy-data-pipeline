Pesticide warning labels.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Strock and Campbell, K and

A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to public health and safety; providing
2 standards for a pesticide manufacturer's or seller's duty
3 to warn consumers or the public about pesticide risks as
4 specified; and providing for an effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 35-7-360(c) and by creating a new
9 subsection (e) is amended to read:
10
11 35-7-360. Liability for damage; warning labels;
12 service of process.
13
14 (c) Except as provided by subsection (e) of this
15 section, nothing in this act shall be construed to relieve

1 any person from liability for any damage to the person or
2 lands of another caused by the use of pesticides even

4 this act.

6 (e) The duty of a manufacturer or seller of a
7 pesticide to warn a consumer or the public about the risks
8 associated with the pesticide shall be presumed to be
9 satisfied if the pesticide bears the label approved by the
10 United States environmental protection agency under the
11 Insecticide, Fungicide and Rodenticide Act, 7 U.S.C. ยง 136
12 et seq., and the pesticide is registered by the department.
13 This presumption may be rebutted only by a showing that the

17 known at the time the pesticide was sold that the required
18 warning was not supported by the weight of scientific
