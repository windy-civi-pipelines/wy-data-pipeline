for
1 AN ACT relating to the department of family services;

3 family services to review the effectiveness and impact of
4 the department's programs; authorizing the sharing of data;
5 creating exceptions for the sharing of confidential
6 information; requiring rulemaking; requiring a report; and
7 providing for effective dates.

1 35-20-108. Records confidential; exception.
2
3 Except as provided under W.S. 9-2-2104(a)(x) and 35-20-116,

21 performance of duties under this article for purposes not
22 directly related to the administration of this article.
