2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0184
School finance-average daily membership.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Harshman and Clouston and
Senator(s) Rothfuss and Schuler
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to school finance; modifying the definition
2 of average daily membership as specified; specifying
3 applicability; and providing for an effective date.
4
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
6
7 Section 1. W.S. 21-13-101(a)(i) is amended to read:
8
9 21-13-101. Definitions.
10
11 (a) As used in this chapter:
12
13 (i) "Average daily membership" or "ADM" means
14 the aggregate number of pupils present plus the aggregate
15 number of pupils absent, divided by the actual number of
1 HB0008
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0184
1 days the school is in session for the year. Pupils who
2 attend at least eighty percent (80%) seventy-five percent
3 (75%) of a full time equivalency basis shall be considered
4 full time. For pupils enrolled in school on less than
5 eighty percent (80%) seventy-five percent (75%) of a full
6 time equivalency basis, the school district shall calculate
7 the pupil's contribution to the ADM on a prorated basis
8 with the hours or class periods of enrollment being the
9 numerator and the hours or class periods of full time
10 equivalency being the denominator. Pupils who have
11 withdrawn from school or who have been absent for more than
12 ten (10) consecutive calendar days shall not be counted as
13 members;
14
15 Section 2. This act shall apply beginning school year
16 2025-2026.
17
18 Section 3. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
