Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0770
Covering loads on Wyoming highways.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Locke
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to motor vehicles; requiring commercial
2 motor vehicles to cover specified loads when driving on
3 highways; and providing for an effective date.
4
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
6
7 Section 1. W.S. 31-18-603 is amended to read:
8
9 31-18-603. Loads on vehicles.
10
11 (a) No vehicle shall be driven or moved on any
12 highway unless the vehicle is so constructed or loaded as
13 to prevent any of its load from dropping, sifting, leaking
14 or otherwise escaping therefrom.
15
1 HB0317
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0770
1 (b) No vehicle with an open bed transporting dirt,
2 gravel, sand or rock shall be driven or moved on any
3 highway unless the open bed is securely covered with a
4 material that will prevent the load from dropping, sifting,
5 leaking or otherwise escaping therefrom.
6
7 (c) This section does not prohibit the necessary
8 spreading of any substance in highway maintenance or
9 construction operations.
10
11 Section 2. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
12
13 (END)
