Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0395
Annual permits for specified commercial loads.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Smith, S, Allemand, Lien,
Neiman and Schmid and Senator(s) Ide and
Kolb
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to motor vehicles; authorizing annual
2 permits for hauling portable building units and building
3 materials; increasing the time permits for hauling forest
4 products, baled hay and combine headers are valid;
5 increasing the width authorized for specified loads;
6 increasing permit fees; removing the width restriction for
7 a single component of the load; providing definitions;
8 requiring rulemaking; and providing for effective dates.
9
10 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
11
12 Section 1. W.S. 31-18-801(a) by creating new
13 paragraphs (xl) and (xli) and 31-18-804(a) are amended to
14 read:
15
1 HB0132
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0395
1 31-18-801. Definitions.
2
3 (a) As used in this article:
4
5 (xl) "Building material" means any material or
6 substance used for construction, renovation or demolition
7 of a structure;
8
9 (xli) "Portable building unit" means a
10 prefabricated structure delivered by a person as a unit.
11 "Portable building unit" includes a fully assembled
12 configuration, a partially assembled configuration and a
13 kit or unassembled configuration when loaded for transport.
14
15 31-18-804. Permits for and certain highways to carry,
16 overweight and oversize vehicles; government vehicles.
17
18 (a) The director or his authorized representative may
19 issue permits for separate movements or extended periods of
20 movement effective for one (1) or more months, not to
21 exceed ninety (90) days one (1) year permitting hauling of
22 forest products, baled hay, portable building units,
23 building materials or combine headers. No permit issued
2 HB0132
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0395
1 under this section shall be granted for any load of forest
2 products exceeding one hundred twelve (112) inches in width
3 or any load of baled hay, portable building units, building
4 materials or combine headers exceeding one hundred fifty
5 (150) one hundred sixty-eight (168) inches in width and
6 fifteen (15) feet in height. No single component of the
7 load may exceed one hundred two (102) inches in width. The
8 fee for a single trip permit is fifteen dollars ($15.00).
9 The fee for an extended period permit is fifty dollars
10 ($50.00) two hundred dollars ($200.00).
11
12 Section 2. The department of transportation shall
13 promulgate all rules necessary to implement this act.
14
3 HB0132
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0395
1 Section 3.
2
3 (a) Except as provided in subsection (b) of this
4 section, this act is effective July 1, 2025.
5
6 (b) Sections 2 and 3 of this act are effective
7 immediately upon completion of all acts necessary for a
8 bill to become law as provided by Article 4, Section 8 of
9 the Wyoming Constitution.
10
11 (END)
