Proof of voter residency-registration qualifications.

A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to elections; requiring a qualified elector
2 to be a bona fide resident of the state of Wyoming for not
3 less than thirty (30) days before the date of the election
4 in which they offer to vote; requiring documentation
5 proving residence to register to vote; prohibiting
6 registration based on documentation showing noncitizen
7 status; making conforming amendments; requiring rulemaking;
8 and providing for effective dates.

8
