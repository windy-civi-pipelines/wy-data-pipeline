Hunting wildlife from vehicles.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Allemand, Angelos and
Johnson and Senator(s) Ide and Laursen, D
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to game and fish; authorizing persons to
2 hunt specified animals from aircraft, automotive vehicles,
3 trailers, motor-propelled wheeled vehicles or snowmobiles;
4 and providing for an effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 23-3-306(a) is amended to read:
9
10 23-3-306. Use of aircraft, automobiles, motorized and
11 snow vehicles and artificial light for hunting or fishing
12 prohibited; exceptions; penalties.
13
14 (a) No person shall harass, pursue, hunt, shoot, or
15 kill any Wyoming wildlife except predatory animals that
1 HB0211
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0360
1 requires a license or permit to take, or is deemed
2 threatened or endangered by Wyoming or federal wildlife
3 regulations, with, from, or by use of any aircraft,
4 automotive vehicle, trailer, motor-propelled wheeled
5 vehicle, or vehicle designed for travel over snow. No
6 person shall use any aircraft, to aid in the taking of any
7 Wyoming wildlife, except predatory animals, whether by
8 spotting or locating the wildlife, communicating with any
9 person attempting to take the wildlife, or by providing
10 other aid to any person taking the wildlife. Nothing in
11 this subsection shall apply to the use of any aircraft by
12 governmental agencies, their employees, contractors or
13 designees performing any lawful duties. The commission may
14 exempt handicapped hunters from any provision of this
15 subsection. For purposes of this section, "aircraft" means
16 any machine or device capable of atmospheric flight
17 including but not limited to an airplane, helicopter,
18 glider, dirigible or unmanned aerial vehicle.
19
20 Section 2. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
