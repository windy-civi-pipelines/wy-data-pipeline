Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0132
SENATE FILE NO. SF0036
Music therapy-use of title.
Committee
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to professions and occupations; authorizing
2 title protection for music therapists; providing
3 exceptions; providing definitions; and providing for an
4 effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 33-50-101 and 33-50-102 are created
9 to read:
10
11 CHAPTER 50
12 MUSIC THERAPISTS
13
14 33-50-101. Definitions.
15
1 SF0036
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0132
1 (a) As used in this chapter:
2
3 (i) "Music therapist" means a person who holds
4 an active music therapist board certified credential
5 granted by the certification board for music therapists;
6
7 (ii) "Music therapy" means the clinical and
8 evidence based use of music interventions to accomplish
9 individualized goals for persons of all ages and ability
10 levels that are delivered during the course of a
11 therapeutic relationship.
12
13 33-50-102. Practice or use of title; exceptions.
14
15 (a) It is unlawful for a person to profess to be a
16 music therapist or advertise in any form or hold themselves
17 out to the public as a music therapist, board certified
18 music therapist or use the initials "MT-BC" or any other
19 words, letters, abbreviations or insignia indicating or
20 implying that the person is a music therapist unless the
21 person holds and maintains a music therapist board
22 certified credential granted by the certification board for
23 music therapists.
2 SF0036
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0132
1
2 (b) This chapter shall not prohibit a person from
3 using music in the performance of the person's profession
4 or occupation if the person does not represent that they
5 are a music therapist.
6
7 Section 2. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
8
9 (END)
