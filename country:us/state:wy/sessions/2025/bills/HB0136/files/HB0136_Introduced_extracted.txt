Volunteer first responder health insurance-revisions.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Byron, Banks, Brown, L,
Davis, Williams and Wylie and Senator(s)
Crago, Dockstader, Gierau, Nethercott and
Schuler
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to compensation and benefits; excluding the
2 Affordable Care Act from government sponsored health care
3 programs that would disqualify a volunteer first responder
4 from participating in the state employees' and officials'
5 group insurance plan; authorizing first responder
6 departments to contribute to first responder group
7 insurance premiums; requiring voluntary first responders to
8 provide proof of their volunteer status on an annual basis;
9 and providing for an effective date.
10
11 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
12
13 Section 1. W.S. 9-3-220(a)(ii), (b) and (c) is
14 amended to read:

2 9-3-220. Volunteer firefighters, volunteer emergency
3 medical technicians and volunteer search and rescue persons
4 participation in the state employees' and officials' group
5 insurance plan; requirements.
6
7 (a) Volunteer firefighters, emergency medical
8 technicians and volunteer search and rescue persons may
9 elect to participate in the state employees' and officials'
10 group insurance plan as defined by W.S. 9-3-203(a)(v) and
11 implemented under W.S. 9-3-202 through 9-3-219. These
12 volunteers are eligible to enroll in the group insurance
13 plan within the first thirty-one (31) days of the enactment
14 of this section or after initially volunteering for ninety
15 (90) days. After the ninety (90) day period is over, the
16 volunteer shall have thirty-one (31) days to enroll in the
17 group insurance plan. Eligible volunteers who elect not to
18 become enrolled within the time prescribed in this
19 subsection may be enrolled at a later date upon conditions
20 the department may impose. Continued participation in the
21 program is limited to a volunteer who:
