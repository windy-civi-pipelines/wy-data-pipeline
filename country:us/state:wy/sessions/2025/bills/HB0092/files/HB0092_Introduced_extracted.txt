Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0119
Wyoming livestock board-memorandums of understanding.
Sponsored by: Joint Agriculture, State and Public Lands &
Water Resources Interim Committee
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to the Wyoming livestock board; requiring
2 the board to initiate memorandums of understanding
3 regarding the identification of livestock delivered to
4 other states; requiring reports; and providing for an
5 effective date.
6
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
8
9 Section 1. The Wyoming livestock board shall initiate
10 memorandums of understanding with the states of California,
11 Colorado, Idaho, Iowa, Kansas, Montana, Nebraska, South
12 Dakota, Utah, Washington and any other state that receives
13 Wyoming livestock. The memorandums of understanding shall
14 establish that livestock may be identified by the methods
15 provided in W.S. 11-18-117(c).
1 HB0092
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0119
1
2 Section 2. The Wyoming livestock board shall provide
3 a status report to the joint agriculture, state and public
4 lands and water resources interim committee on the progress
5 of developing memorandums of understanding with the states
6 listed in section 1 of this act on or before January 1,
7 2026 and on or before June 30, 2026.
8
9 Section 3. This act is effective immediately upon
10 completion of all acts necessary for a bill to become law
11 as provided by Article 4, Section 8 of the Wyoming
12 Constitution.
13
14 (END)
