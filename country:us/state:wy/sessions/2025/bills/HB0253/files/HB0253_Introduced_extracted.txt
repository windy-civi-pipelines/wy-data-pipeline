6
7 Section 1. W.S. 29-2-112(a)(ii)(B) and (iii) is
8 amended to read:
9
10 29-2-112. Preliminary notices.
11
12 (a) With respect to perfecting the right to file a
13 construction lien under this chapter, the following
14 preliminary notice requirements shall apply:
1 HB0253
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0736
1
2 (ii) Any notice required under this section
3 shall be sent:
4
5 (B) By the subcontractor or materialman

7 materials to the construction project before final payment
8 by the owner to the general contractor.
9
10 (iii) Except as otherwise provided in this
11 paragraph, failure to send the notice required under this
12 section within the time specified shall bar the right of a
13 contractor, subcontractor or materialman to assert a lien.
14 A subcontractor or materialman shall not be barred from
15 asserting a lien under this paragraph if the contractor
16 failed to provide the information required under W.S.
17 29-2-113 to the subcontractor or materialman;
