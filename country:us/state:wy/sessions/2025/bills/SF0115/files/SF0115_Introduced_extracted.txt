2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0660
SENATE FILE NO. SF0115
Judicial review of agency actions-limitations.
Representative(s) Andrew, Byron and Erickson
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to administrative procedure; specifying
2 limitations on the judicial review of agency actions;
3 specifying applicability; and providing for an effective
4 date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 16-3-114(a) is amended to read:
9
10 16-3-114. Judicial review of agency actions; district
11 courts.
12
13 (a) Subject to the requirement that administrative
14 remedies be exhausted and in the absence of any statutory
15 or common-law provision precluding or limiting judicial
1 SF0115
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0660
1 review, Any person aggrieved or adversely affected in fact
2 by a final decision of an agency in a contested case, or by
3 other agency action or inaction, or any person affected in
4 fact by a rule adopted by an agency, is entitled to
5 judicial review, subject to all of the following:
6
7 (i) Judicial review shall be in the district
8 court for the county in which the injury or harm for which
9 relief is sought occurred, in the district court for the
10 county in which the administrative action or inaction was
11 taken, or in which any real property affected by the
12 administrative action or inaction is located, or if no real
13 property is involved, in the district court for the county
14 in which the party aggrieved or adversely affected by the
15 administrative action or inaction resides or has its
16 principal place of business;.
17
18 (ii) The procedure to be followed in the
19 proceeding before the district court shall be in accordance
20 with rules heretofore or hereinafter adopted by the Wyoming
21 supreme court;
22
2 SF0115
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0660
1 (iii) All administrative remedies shall be
2 exhausted before a person seeks judicial review;
3
4 (iv) There is no statutory or common-law
5 provision that precludes or limits judicial review;
6
7 (v) The right of judicial review under this
8 subsection shall not apply to any action or inaction taken
9 in regards to the public employment of a person, unless:
10
11 (A) The action or inaction is the final
12 decision of an agency in a contested case; or
13
14 (B) Judicial review for the action or
15 inaction is otherwise provided by law.
16
17 Section 2. This act shall apply to agency actions and
18 inactions taken on and after July 1, 2025.
19
20 Section 3. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
