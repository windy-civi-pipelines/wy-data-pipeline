Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0086
SENATE FILE NO. SF0066
Digital ledger filing system development.
Technology and Digital Innovation Technology
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to the office of the secretary of state;
2 requiring the University of Wyoming center for blockchain
3 and digital innovation in collaboration with the secretary
4 of state to develop a blockchain or other distributed
5 ledger filing system; providing recommendations for the
6 filing system; providing definitions; authorizing the
7 promulgation of rules; creating an account; providing a
8 funding source for the account; providing an appropriation;
9 and providing for an effective date.
10
11 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
12
13 Section 1.
14
1 SF0066
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0086
1 (a) Subject to subsection (c) of this section, the
2 University of Wyoming center for blockchain and digital
3 innovation in collaboration with the secretary of state
4 shall develop a filing system through which all required
5 filings, as defined in paragraph (d)(iii) of this section,
6 may be submitted and:
7
8 (i) Blockchain technology or other distributed
9 ledger technology shall be the technological framework for
10 the filing system. This may include an application
11 programming interface, security measures, authenticated
12 digital corporate identity and other components determined
13 by the secretary of state to be best practices and likely
14 to increase the effective and efficient administration of
15 the laws of this state;
16
17 (ii) To the extent reasonably possible, the
18 filing system shall be designed to accept existing and
19 future blockchain or other distributed ledger technology-
20 based filings including a digital asset lien registry,
21 digital identity registration and digital asset
22 registration;
23
2 SF0066
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0086
1 (iii) The University of Wyoming center for
2 blockchain and digital innovation may develop a blockchain
3 or other distributed ledger technology for the purposes of
4 this section or contract for the use of a privately created
5 blockchain or other distributed ledger technology as
6 necessary.
7
8 (b) The University of Wyoming center for blockchain
9 and digital innovation and the secretary of state may:
10
11 (i) Consult with interested parties before
12 developing the filing system required by subsection (a) of
13 this section, including businesses, registered agents,
14 attorneys, law enforcement and other interested persons;
15 and
16
17 (ii) If necessary, partner with technology
18 innovators and private companies to develop necessary
19 components of the filing system.
20
21 (c) The secretary of state may allow for the testing
22 and implementation of the filing system developed pursuant
23 to this act at the secretary's discretion. Nothing in this
3 SF0066
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0086
1 act shall require the secretary of state to implement the
2 filing system developed pursuant to this act.
3
4 (d) As used in this section:
5
6 (i) "Application programming interface" means a
7 computer software intermediary or protocol that allows two
8 (2) or more distinct software applications to interact;
9
10 (ii) "Blockchain" means a digital ledger or
11 database that is chronological, consensus-based,
12 decentralized and mathematically verified in nature;
13
14 (iii) "Required filings" means all documents,
15 reports, data and other information required by law to be
16 filed with the secretary of state pursuant to title 17,
17 title 34.1, title 34 or title 40 of the Wyoming statutes.
18
19 Section 2. The secretary of state may adopt rules to
20 implement this act.
21
22 Section 3.
23
4 SF0066
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0086
1 (a) Beginning on April 1, 2025 the first two million
2 dollars ($2,000,000.00) collected under W.S. 17-16-1630
3 shall be deposited in the digital ledger filing system
4 account which is hereby created.
5
6 (b) There is appropriated two million dollars
7 ($2,000,000.00) from the digital ledger filing system
8 account to the University of Wyoming center for blockchain
9 and digital innovation for developing the filing system
10 required by this act. This appropriation shall be for the
11 period beginning with the effective date of this act and
12 ending June 30, 2027. Funds from the account will be
13 distributed quarterly as they become available until the
14 full amount appropriated under this subsection is
15 distributed. This appropriation shall not be transferred or
16 expended for any other purpose and any unexpended,
17 unobligated funds remaining from this appropriation shall
18 revert to the general fund on June 30, 2027.
19

3 as provided by Article 4, Section 8 of the Wyoming
