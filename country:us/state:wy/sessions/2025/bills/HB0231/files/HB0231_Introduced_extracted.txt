Medical education funding.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Larsen, L and Nicholas and
Senator(s) Dockstader and Landen
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to the University of Wyoming; providing an
2 appropriation; authorizing expansion of the course of
3 training and education offered in the field of medicine as
4 specified; and providing for an effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1.
9
10 (a) There is appropriated two million one hundred
11 seventy-six thousand three hundred thirteen dollars
12 ($2,176,313.00) from the general fund to the University of
13 Wyoming for purposes of providing medical education under
14 this section. Not more than fifty-three thousand eighty-one
15 dollars ($53,081.00) of this appropriation shall be
1 HB0231
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0624
1 expended for administrative costs incurred by the
2 University of Wyoming in providing medical education under
3 this section. This appropriation shall be for the period
4 beginning with the effective date of this section and
5 ending June 30, 2029. This appropriation shall not be
6 transferred or expended for any other purpose.
7 Notwithstanding W.S. 9-2-1008, 9-2-1012(e) and 9-4-207, any
8 unexpended, unobligated funds from this appropriation shall
9 revert as provided by law on June 30, 2028. It is the
10 intent of the legislature that this appropriation be
11 doubled and included in the University of Wyoming's
12 standard budget for the immediately succeeding fiscal
13 biennium.
14
15 (b) The board of trustees of the University of
16 Wyoming is authorized to expand the course of training and
17 education offered by the university in the field of
18 medicine under a contract with the university of Utah
19 school of medicine as specified in subsection (c) of this
20 section and in accordance with W.S. 21-17-109.
21
2 HB0231
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0624
1 (c) Medical training and education offered by the
2 University of Wyoming under a contract with the university
3 of Utah shall be subject to all of the following:
4
5 (i) Medical education shall be available for not
6 more than five (5) Wyoming resident students for a period
7 of four (4) years, commencing with academic year 2025‑2026;
8
9 (ii) The board of trustees of the University of
10 Wyoming shall obtain agreements from each student as
11 required by W.S. 21-17-109(d);
12
13 (iii) Medical education shall be subject to all
14 requirements of W.S. 21-17-109.
15
16 Section 2. This act is effective immediately upon
17 completion of all acts necessary for a bill to become law
18 as provided by Article 4, Section 8 of the Wyoming
19 Constitution.
20 (END)
