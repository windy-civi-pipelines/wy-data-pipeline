1 AN ACT relating to ad valorem taxation; amending how

3 statements are paid and credited; and providing for an
4 effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 39-13-108(c)(ii)(B) is amended to
9 read:
10
11 39-13-108. Enforcement.
12
13 (c) Offenses and penalties. The following shall
14 apply:
15
16 (ii) Penalties. The following shall apply:
1 HB0038
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0105
1
2 (B) Any person failing to file the
3 statement provided by W.S. 39-13-107(a)(ii)(A) or (B) is
4 subject to a penalty of not more than five hundred dollars
5 ($500.00) plus not more than one hundred dollars ($100.00)
6 for each day's failure to file the statement, to be
7 recovered by an action in the name of the state of Wyoming
8 brought by the attorney general at the request of the
9 department. The department may waive the penalties imposed
10 by this subsection as part of a settlement or for any other
11 good cause shown. Penalties collected shall be credited
12 paid to the state school foundation program account
13 treasurer and credited as provided in W.S. 8-1-109;
14
15 Section 2. This act is effective immediately upon

17 as provided by Article 4, Section 8 of the Wyoming
