2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0057
Hunting license application fees increase.
Sponsored by: Joint Travel, Recreation, Wildlife & Cultural
Resources Interim Committee
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to game and fish; authorizing the game and
2 fish commission to set application fees for hunting
3 licenses as specified; removing minimum contribution
4 requirements to property damage compensation fund;
5 requiring rulemaking; and providing for effective dates.
6
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
8
9 Section 1. W.S. 23-2-101(e) is amended to read:
10
11 23-2-101. Fees; restrictions; nonresident application
12 fee; nonresident licenses; verification of residency
13 required; donation of refunded application fees.
14
1 HB0002
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0057
1 (e) Resident and nonresident license applicants shall
2 pay an application fee in an amount specified by this
3 subsection upon submission of an application for purchase
4 of any limited quota drawing for big or trophy game license
5 or wild bison license. The resident Application fee fees
6 shall be five dollars ($5.00) established by rule and shall
7 not exceed twenty dollars ($20.00) for resident
8 applications and the seventy-five dollars ($75.00) for
9 nonresident application fee shall be fifteen dollars
10 ($15.00) applications for all personal applications made at
11 the same time. The application fee is in addition to the
12 fees prescribed by subsections (f) and (j) of this section
13 and by W.S. 23-2-107 and shall be payable to the department
14 either directly or through an authorized selling agent of
15 the department. At the beginning of each month, the
16 commission shall set aside all of the fees collected during
17 calendar year 1980 and not to exceed twenty-five percent
18 (25%) of the fees collected thereafter pursuant to this
19 subsection to establish and maintain a working balance of
20 five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000.00), to compensate
21 owners or lessees of property damaged by game animals and
22 game birds.
