Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0796
Maintenance of voter lists.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Lucas, Brady, Brown, G,
Campbell, K, Guggenmos, Hoeft, Johnson,
Smith, S, Webb and Webber
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to elections; clarifying the duties of the
2 secretary of state related to verification of voter
3 registration data; specifying when notice of intent to
4 cancel a registration may be provided; and providing for an
5 effective date.
6
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
8
9 Section 1. W.S. 22-3-102(e)(i), by creating a new
10 paragraph (vi) and by creating a new subsection (f) and
11 22-3-116 are amended to read:
12
13 22-3-102. Qualifications; temporary registration.
14
1 HB0318
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0796
1 (e) The secretary of state is authorized to provide
2 for the verification of certain voter registration data in
3 accordance with the following:
4
5 (i) The secretary of state and the director of
6 the department of transportation shall enter into an
7 agreement to match voter registration data with information
8 maintained by the department regarding driver's licenses,
9 in order to verify the information provided on applications
10 for voter registration, determine if any alien
11 identification number or other evidence of non-United
12 States citizenship is kept within corresponding driver's
13 license records, determine if any voters listed in the
14 Wyoming voter registration system have subsequently
15 received a driver's license from another state as indicated
16 by information received from the state-to-state
17 verification service operated by the American association
18 of motor vehicle administrators or verify any other
19 information indicating a voter should be ineligible to vote
20 at the request of the secretary of state;
21
22 (vi) The secretary of state may utilize the
23 systematic alien verification for entitlements (SAVE)
2 HB0318
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0796
1 program operated by the United States citizenship and
2 immigration services for purposes of verifying that voters
3 listed in the Wyoming voter registration system are United
4 States citizens.
5
6 (f) The department of transportation shall furnish
7 monthly to the secretary of state a complete list of all
8 persons who have indicated a noncitizen status in obtaining
9 any document, or renewal thereof, issued pursuant to W.S.
10 31-7-115 and 31-8-102. The secretary of state may transmit
11 the information from the list to appropriate county clerks.
12 Information in these lists shall be kept confidential and
13 available only for official use.
14
15 22-3-116. Notice of intent to cancel registration.
16
17 When the county clerk has information that a registration
18 should be cancelled, he shall mail a notice of intent to
19 cancel to the elector at his address on the registry list
20 stating the reason for cancellation. When the secretary of
21 state or county clerk receives information indicating that
22 there is evidence that an elector may have moved to another
23 state, the secretary of state and the county clerk may use
3 HB0318
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0796
1 any lawful means to investigate whether the registration
2 should be cancelled, and based on this investigation, the
3 county clerk may mail a notice of intent to cancel to the
4 elector in accordance with this section. The notice shall
5 state that cancellation shall occur within twenty (20) days
6 unless the elector asks that his name remain on the
7 registry list. A copy of the notice of cancellation shall
8 be retained by the county clerk for three (3) years. A
9 notice is not required if the clerk has received a receipt
10 of notification that the elector has registered to vote in
11 another jurisdiction or if the elector requested
12 cancellation in writing.
13
14 Section 2. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
15
16 (END)
