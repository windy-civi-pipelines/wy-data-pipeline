2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0423
Wind tax exemption-repeal.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Johnson, Angelos, Banks,
Brown, G, Campbell, K, Guggenmos, Harshman,
Hoeft, Knapp, Locke, Lucas, Singh, Strock,
Styvar and Wasserburger and Senator(s)
Brennan, Case, Hutchings and Pearson
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to the tax on production of energy from
2 wind resources; repealing the three (3) year tax exemption
3 on wind energy production; specifying applicability; and
4 providing for an effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 39-22-105(b) is repealed.
9
10 Section 2. The three (3) year tax exemption provided
11 in W.S. 39‑22‑105(b), repealed by section 1 of this act,
12 shall apply in accordance with the terms of that repealed
13 subsection to any wind turbine that first produced
14 electricity for sale before the effective date of this act.

1
2 Section 3. This act is effective immediately upon
3 completion of all acts necessary for a bill to become law
4 as provided by Article 4, Section 8 of the Wyoming
5 Constitution.

7 (END)
