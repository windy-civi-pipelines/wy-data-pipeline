Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0648
Utilities-point of consumption and allocation agreements.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Williams, Davis, Erickson,
Haroldson, Lawley and Neiman and Senator(s)
Barlow, Cooper, Driskill, Gierau and
Steinmetz
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to public utilities; requiring customer
2 allocation agreements for utility service to a customer in
3 the certificated territory of another utility; authorizing
4 a civil action and recovery for violations; and providing
5 for an effective date.
6
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
8
9 Section 1. W.S. 37-2-205 by creating a new subsection
10 (k) is amended to read:
11
12 37-2-205. Certificate of convenience and necessity;
13 hearings.
14
1 HB0184
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0648
1 (k) No person or entity shall sell electricity for
2 use by a customer in another public utility's certificated
3 service territory, including by providing service to a
4 point of delivery outside of the other public utility's
5 certificated territory for consumption within the other
6 public utility's certificated territory, without a customer
7 allocation agreement between the customer, the seller of
8 the electricity and the other public utility. Any violation
9 of this subsection shall entitle the aggrieved public
10 utility to recover in a civil action damages for lost
11 income from the unlawful invasion of its certificated
12 service territory, plus all reasonable attorney fees and
13 expenses incurred by the aggrieved public utility in the
14 civil action brought under this subsection.
15
16 Section 2. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
17
18 (END)
