Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0176
Limited mining operations-water quality testing.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Harshman, Campbell, E and
Larson, JT and Senator(s) Anderson, Boner
and Rothfuss
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to environmental quality; specifying that
2 the department of environmental quality may conduct water
3 quality testing before the commencement of limited mining
4 operations; amending notice requirements for limited mining
5 operations; requiring rulemaking; and providing for an
6 effective date.
7
8 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
9
10 Section 1. W.S. 35-11-401(e)(vi)(intro) and by
11 creating a new subparagraph (H) is amended to read:
12
13 35-11-401. Compliance generally; exceptions.
14
1 HB0059
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0176
1 (e) The provisions of this article shall not apply to
2 any of the following activities:
3
4 (vi) Limited mining operations, whether
5 commercial or noncommercial, for the removal of sand,
6 gravel, scoria, limestone, dolomite, shale, ballast or
7 feldspar from an area of fifteen (15) acres or less of
8 affected land, excluding roads used to access the mining
9 operation, if the operator has written permission for the
10 operation from the owner and lessee, if any, of the
11 surface. The operator shall notify the land quality
12 division and the water quality division of the department
13 of environmental quality and the inspector of mines within
14 the department of workforce services of the location of the
15 land to be mined and the postal address of the operator at
16 least thirty (30) days before commencing operations. A copy
17 of the notice shall also be mailed to all surface owners
18 located within one (1) mile of the proposed boundary of the
19 limited mining operation at least thirty (30) days before
20 commencing operations. The operator shall notify the land
21 quality division of the department of environmental quality
22 of the date of commencement of limited mining operations
23 within thirty (30) days of commencing operations. Limited
2 HB0059
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0176
1 mining operations authorized under this paragraph are
2 subject to the following:
3
4 (H) The department may require water
5 quality testing before the operator commences limited
6 mining operations if the department determines or has
7 reason to believe that any domestic or drinking water
8 source may be impacted by the limited mining operations.
9 The administrator may require the operator to delay the
10 commencement of the limited mining operations for a
11 reasonable time in order to complete any water quality
12 testing required under this subparagraph.
13
14 Section 2. The environmental quality council, upon
15 recommendation by the department of environmental quality,
16 shall promulgate all rules necessary to implement this act.
17
18 Section 3. This act is effective immediately upon
19 completion of all acts necessary for a bill to become law
20 as provided by Article 4, Section 8 of the Wyoming
21 Constitution.
22
23 (END)
