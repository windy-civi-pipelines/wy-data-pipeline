3 "(H) Before an operator commences a limited mining
4 operation, the department may require the operator to test the
5 water quality of domestic wells within three hundred (300) feet of
6 the proposed limited mining operation for persons identified by
7 the administrator of the water quality division of the department.
8 The department shall not require the operator to sample any
9 domestic wells that the well owner does not grant permission for
10 the operator to sample. The operator shall submit water quality
11 testing results to the administrator of the land quality division
12 of the department.". HEINER, CHAIRMAN
