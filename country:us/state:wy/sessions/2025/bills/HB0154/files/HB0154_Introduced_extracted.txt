2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0575
False voting-amendments.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Brady, Bear, Heiner, Lien,
McCann and Neiman and Senator(s) Pearson
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to elections; clarifying that the false
2 voting statute includes out-of-state voting; authorizing
3 the secretary of state to request an investigation; and
4 providing for an effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 22-26-106(a)(iv) and (e) is amended
9 to read:
10
11 22-26-106. False voting.
12
13 (a) False voting consists of performing any of the
14 following acts in connection with or related to the
15 election process or an election:
1 HB0154
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0575
1
2 (iv) Voting, or offering to vote, more than once
3 in an election, including voting more than once in a
4 Wyoming election, or voting in any other state and then
5 voting, or offering to vote, in Wyoming for the same
6 federal election.
7
8 (e) The county sheriff shall investigate acts of
9 false voting at the request of the secretary of state or a
10 county clerk who has reasonable cause to believe that a
11 person has committed false voting. After an investigation
12 and a finding that the allegation has merit, the county
13 sheriff shall refer the matter to the district attorney for
14 prosecution in the appropriate courts of this state.
15
16 Section 2. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
17
18 (END)
