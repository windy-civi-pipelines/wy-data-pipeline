2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0490
State auditor payment transparency.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Bratten, Hoeft, Knapp,
Lawley, Locke, Lucas and Webb and Senator(s)
Boner, Hutchings and Ide
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to the administration of the government;
2 requiring that the state auditor provide the public with
3 information on payments made to vendors as specified;
4 specifying applicability; and providing for an effective
5 date.
6
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
8
9 Section 1. W.S. 9-1-403(a) by creating a new
10 paragraph (ix) is amended to read:
11
12 9-1-403. State auditor; duties; prohibited acts;
13 powers; investigative subpoenas.
14
15 (a) The state auditor shall:
1 HB0166
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0490
1
2 (ix) Subject to state and federal law, make
3 available for public inspection and download on the state
4 auditor's official website information on all payments
5 within the purview of the state auditor's office made to
6 vendors for items furnished or services rendered. The
7 information shall include, at a minimum, the date of each
8 payment, the vendor's name, the state agency requiring
9 items or services or from whose funds the payment is made,
10 the total amount paid and a description of the payment.
11 Payment information required under this paragraph shall be
12 posted to the state auditor's official website not later
13 than thirty (30) days after the payment is made by the
14 state auditor. Information published on the official
15 website under this paragraph shall be maintained on the
16 website for not less than five (5) years.
17
18 Section 2. This act shall apply to all payments made
19 by the state auditor on and after July 1, 2025.
20
21 Section 3. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
22
23 (END)
