2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0740
SENATE FILE NO. SF0170
Grizzly bear management prohibition.
Sponsored by: Senator(s) Hicks and Driskill and
Representative(s) Hoeft and Winter
for
1 AN ACT relating to game and fish; limiting the actions the
2 game and fish department and commission may take in the
3 management of grizzly bears; requiring rulemaking; and
4 providing for an effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 23-1-302 by creating a new subsection
9 (s) is amended to read:
10
11 23-1-302. Powers and duties.
12
13 (s) The commission and department shall not take or
14 engage in any management actions or activities for grizzly
15 bears unless the action is expressly required by statute or
1 SF0170
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0740
1 the action is necessary for the public safety and welfare.
2 This subsection shall apply until the date on which the
3 grizzly bear in Wyoming is delisted under the federal
4 Endangered Species Act and primary management of the
5 grizzly bear is returned to the state.
6
7 Section 2. The game and fish commission shall
8 promulgate all rules necessary to implement this act.
9
10 Section 3. This act is effective immediately upon
11 completion of all acts necessary for a bill to become law
12 as provided by Article 4, Section 8 of the Wyoming
13 Constitution.
14
15 (END)
