Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0695
Ballot initiatives-duplicate laws passed.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Lien, Brady, Johnson and
Lucas
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to elections; removing a provision allowing
2 for ballot proposition initiatives to be deemed void and
3 not placed on the election ballot as specified; and
4 providing for an effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 22-24-319(a)(iii) is amended to read:
9
10 22-24-319. Ballot proposition; when placed on ballot.
11
12 (a) The ballot proposition for an initiative shall be
13 placed on the election ballot of the first statewide
14 general election that is held after:
15
1 HB0196
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0695
1 (iii) A period of one hundred twenty (120) days
2 has expired since the adjournment of the legislative
3 session. If the attorney general determines that an act of
4 the legislature enacted after the petition is filed is
5 substantially the same as the proposed law, the petition
6 shall be void and the ballot proposition shall not be
7 placed on the election ballot, and the secretary of state
8 shall so notify the committee of applicants.
9
10 Section 2. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
11
12 (END)
