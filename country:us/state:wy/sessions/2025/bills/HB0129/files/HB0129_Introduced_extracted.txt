for
1 AN ACT relating to school finance; removing an additional

6
7 Section 1. W.S. 21-13-207 is amended to read:
8
9 21-13-207. Apportionment of funds by county
10 treasurer.
11
12 On the second Monday of each month, and on June 20 or the
13 last business day immediately preceding June 20 of each
14 year, the county treasurer shall apportion all monies in
15 the county treasury belonging to the county school fund,

2 and forfeitures, among the various school districts of the

6
7 Section 2. This act is effective immediately upon
