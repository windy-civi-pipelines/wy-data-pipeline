Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0250
Multi-family dwelling single stairwell exits.
Sponsored by: Joint Appropriations Committee
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to public health and safety; requiring
2 counties, cities and towns to allow single exits in
3 specified multi-family dwellings; providing exceptions; and
4 providing for an effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 16-13-101 is created to read:
9
10 CHAPTER 13
11 BUILDING CODES
12
13 16-13-101. Single exits.
14
15 (a) Except as otherwise provided in subsection (b) of
16 this section and notwithstanding any other law to the
1 HB0088
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0250
1 contrary, no state agency, county, city or town shall
2 require more than one (1) exit to serve six (6) or fewer
3 stories of a residential group R-2 occupancy. For purposes
4 of this section, "Residential group R-2 occupancy" means a
5 residential occupancy containing sleeping units or more
6 than two (2) dwelling units where the occupants are
7 primarily permanent in nature including apartment houses,
8 boarding houses, convents, dormitories and work units.
9
10 (b) A county, city or town may adopt an ordinance or
11 resolution requiring more than one (1) exit to six (6) or
12 fewer stories of a residential group R-2 occupancy in the
13 same building provided that the adoption of a uniform code
14 such as the international building code shall not be deemed
15 to prohibit one (1) exit unless, in addition to adopting a
16 uniform code, the ordinance or resolution expressly
17 requires more than one (1) exit to serve six (6) or fewer
18 stories of a residential group R-2 occupancy.
19
20 Section 2. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
21
22 (END)
