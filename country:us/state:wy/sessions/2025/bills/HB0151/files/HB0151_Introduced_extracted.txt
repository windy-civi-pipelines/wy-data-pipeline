Predator management district appointments and terms.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Lien
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to predator management districts; amending
2 predator management advisory board director term periods;
3 requiring sportsman and hunter directors to have held a
4 valid fishing or hunting license within the previous
5 twenty-four months; removing staggered term limits for
6 sportsmen and hunter directors; and providing for an
7 effective date.
8
9 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
10
