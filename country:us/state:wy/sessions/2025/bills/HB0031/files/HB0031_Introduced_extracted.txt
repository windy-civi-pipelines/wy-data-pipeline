2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0147
School safety and security.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Pendergraft, Bratten,
Brown, L, Clouston, Johnson, Kelly and Webb
and Senator(s) Rothfuss
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to education; requiring school districts to
2 adopt school safety and security policies; creating duties
3 for the state fire marshal; providing confidentiality
4 requirements; and providing for an effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 21-3-137 is created to read:
9
10 21-3-137. School safety and security policies.
11
12 (a) Each school district board of trustees shall
13 adopt safety and security policies, including threat
14 assessment procedures.
15
1 HB0031
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0147
1 (b) The superintendent of each school district shall
2 implement the safety and security policies adopted under
3 subsection (a) of this section.
4
5 (c) A school district's safety and security policies
6 and drafts of the policies shall be confidential and shall
7 not be deemed a public record available for public
8 inspection under the Public Records Act. Each school
9 district board of trustees may develop, discuss and review
10 safety and security policies under this section in
11 executive session not open to the public under W.S.
12 16-4-405(a)(ix) and (xii).
13
14 Section 2. W.S. 21-3-110(a) by creating a new
15 paragraph (xliv) and 35-9-107 by creating a new subsection
16 (d) are amended to read:
17
18 21-3-110. Duties of boards of trustees.
19
20 (a) The board of trustees in each school district
21 shall:
22
2 HB0031
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0147
1 (xliv) Adopt school safety and security policies
2 in accordance with W.S. 21-3-137.
3
4 35-9-107. Duties and powers of state fire marshal.
5
6 (d) The state fire marshal shall not prohibit the use
7 of a door securement device used under a school safety and
8 security policy adopted under W.S. 21-3-137. The state fire
9 marshal may adopt rules regulating the use of door
10 securement devices, including requiring that door
11 securement devices only be deployed to address active
12 threats or during safety drills under W.S. 35-9-505.
13
14 Section 3. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
15
16 (END)
