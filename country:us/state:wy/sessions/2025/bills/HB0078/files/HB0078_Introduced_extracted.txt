2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0507
Newborn safety device funding for safe haven providers.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Rodriguez-Williams and
Angelos and Senator(s) Brennan and Steinmetz
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to safety for a newborn child; authorizing
2 a grant program for safe haven providers to purchase
3 newborn safety devices as specified; providing definitions;
4 requiring rulemaking; providing an appropriation; and
5 providing for effective dates.
6
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
8
9 Section 1.
10
11 (a) As used in this act:
12
13
14 (i) "Newborn child" means as defined in W.S.
15 14-11-102(a)(vi);
1 HB0078
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0507
1
2 (ii) "Newborn safety device" means a device
3 installed at a safe haven provider where a newborn child
4 can be safely placed and that alerts the personnel of the
5 safe haven provider that a newborn child has been placed in
6 the device;
7
8 (iii) "Safe haven provider" means as defined in
9 W.S. 14-11-102(a)(vii).
10
11 (b) The department of family services shall establish
12 and administer a grant program for the purpose of providing
13 grants for safe haven providers to purchase newborn safety
14 devices. The safe haven providers that receive grants under
15 this act shall manage the newborn safety devices. Each safe
16 haven provider shall receive not more than one (1) grant
17 and one (1) newborn safety device.
18
19 (c) The department of family services shall develop
20 an application process for safe haven providers to apply
21 for and receive grants to purchase newborn safety devices
22 and shall review applications to determine eligibility. Any
23 safe haven provider may apply for a grant under this
2 HB0078
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0507
1 section pursuant to the application process established by
2 the department of family services.
3
4 (d) The temporary grant program shall terminate June
5 30, 2026. Applications shall be received not later than
6 March 1, 2026. No grants shall be awarded under this
7 section after June 30, 2026.
8
9 Section 2. There is appropriated three hundred
10 thousand dollars ($300,000.00) from the general fund to the
11 department of family services for purposes of implementing
12 this act. This appropriation shall be for the period
13 beginning July 1, 2025 and ending June 30, 2026. This
14 appropriation shall only be used to make grants to safe
15 haven providers to purchase newborn safety devices, except
16 that not more than one percent (1%) of this appropriation
17 may be used for administrative expenses incurred pursuant
18 to this act. This appropriation shall not be transferred or
19 expended for any other purpose and any unexpended,
20 unobligated funds remaining from this appropriation on June
21 30, 2026 shall revert as provided by law.
22
