Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0385
Minimum easement standards.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Banks and Allemand and
Senator(s) Laursen, D and Steinmetz
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to eminent domain; requiring notice of
2 condemnation as specified; requiring clauses relating to
3 liability, abandonment and reclamation; providing
4 requirements for existing easements; providing compensation
5 standards; specifying applicability; and providing for an
6 effective date.
7
8 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
9
10 Section 1. W.S. 1-26-517 is created to read:
11
12 1-26-517. Minimum easement standards.
13
14 (a) Each person shall comply with the requirements of
15 this section before acquiring any property by condemnation.
1 HB0119
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0385
1
2 (b) Not less than thirty (30) days before a
3 condemnation action is commenced under this act, the
4 condemnor shall give notice to the board of county
5 commissioners of the county and any local governing body of
6 a city or town in which the condemnation is to take place.
7
8 (c) Any easement established under this act shall
9 indicate that the original landowner is not responsible for
10 any liability, including vicarious liability, other than
11 intentional acts arising from use of the land under the
12 easement.
13
14 (d) The condemnor shall be responsible for
15 reclamation of the property after the condemnor no longer
16 requires the easement, pursuant to W.S. 1‑26‑714.
17
18 (e) An existing easement shall be used exclusively
19 for its stated purpose unless the existing easement:
20
21 (i) Contemplates a method by which an easement
22 can be enlarged or amended; or
23
2 HB0119
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0385
1 (ii) Authorizes the parties to renegotiate the
2 easement.
3
4 (f) For purposes of calculating compensation,
5 improvements to the property shall be included in its fair
6 market value.
7
8 (g) If an easement is abandoned pursuant to W.S.
9 1‑26‑515, the land shall revert to the original landowner
10 and the land shall be reclaimed pursuant to W.S. 1‑26‑714
11 and at the expense of the condemnor.
12
13 Section 2. This act applies to condemnation actions
14 initiated on or after July 1, 2025. Nothing in this act
15 shall be construed to impair any existing contracts.
16
17 Section 3. This act is effective immediately upon
18 completion of all acts necessary for a bill to become law
19 as provided by Article 4, Section 8 of the Wyoming
20 Constitution.
21
22 (END)
