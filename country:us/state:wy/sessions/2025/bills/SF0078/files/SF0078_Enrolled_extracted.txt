Section 2:
Section 1. W.S. 22-9-104 by creating a new subsection
(e) is amended to read:
22-9-104. How to apply; information required.
(e) No person, except a county clerk, the secretary
of state or their designees, shall distribute an absentee
ballot request form to any qualified elector unless that
qualified elector specifically solicits an absentee ballot
request form. For purposes of this subsection, "absentee
ballot request form" includes any document that claims to
be or that is deceptively similar to an official election
document and that is provided to the qualified elector by
mail, email or any other means.
