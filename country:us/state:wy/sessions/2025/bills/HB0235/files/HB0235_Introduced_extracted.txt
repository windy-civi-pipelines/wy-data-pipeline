Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0685
Career technical education equipment and supplies.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Andrew, Bear, Campbell, K,
Erickson, Lawley, Neiman, Strock, Williams
and Wylie and Senator(s) Boner, Brennan,
Crum, Driskill, Hicks, Kolb and Scott
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to public school finance; modifying
2 resources provided through the education resource block
3 grant model for career technical education equipment and
4 supplies; requiring resources generated for career
5 technical education supplies, materials and equipment be
6 expended as specified; creating a categorical grant for
7 career technical education supplies, materials and
8 equipment; making conforming amendments; making technical
9 corrections; repealing provisions; appropriating funds; and
10 providing for an effective date.
11
12 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
13
14 Section 1. W.S. 21-13-338 is created to read:
1 HB0235
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0685
1
2 21-13-338. Career and technical education supplies,
3 materials and equipment; criteria; limitations.
4
5 (a) Commencing with school year 2026-2027, to augment
6 amounts within the education resource block grant model
7 available to districts for career and technical education
8 programs, a school district may apply to the department of
9 education for career and technical education supplies,
10 materials and equipment funding in accordance with this
11 section. Application shall be on a form and in a manner
12 prescribed by the department.
13
14 (b) Each school district shall report the number of
15 full-time equivalent (FTE) career and technical education
16 teachers within a school from the prior school year, as
17 computed in accordance with guidelines prescribed by the
18 department, who are providing career and technical
19 education instruction in grades nine (9) through twelve
20 (12) and except as provided under W.S. 21-2-202(a)(xxvii),
21 certified by the Wyoming professional teaching standards
22 board to provide instruction at the high school level for
23 career and technical education courses comprising career
2 HB0235
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0685
1 and technical education programs. Nothing in this
2 subsection shall require a district to employ teachers
3 certified for high school career and technical education
4 instruction on a full-time basis or to require teachers to
5 teach only high school career and technical education
6 courses on a full-time basis.
7
8 (c) Amounts awarded to each school district for
9 career and technical education supplies, materials and
10 equipment funding under this section shall be calculated as
11 the product of each school's FTE career and technical
12 education teachers reported under subsection (b) of this
13 section multiplied by fourteen thousand one hundred
14 eighty-four dollars and sixty-eight cents ($14,184.68). The
15 department shall further adjust the amount under this
16 subsection by any external cost adjustment enacted by the
17 legislature for the educational materials category of the
18 educational resource block grant model.
19
20 (d) Not later than October 15 of the applicable
21 school year and following review of applications submitted
22 under this section, the department shall notify applicant
23 districts of awards and shall provide each applicant
3 HB0235
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0685
1 district a written statement of the reasons for approving
2 or denying the application. Upon approval of applications,
3 the department shall pay each school district not later
4 than February 15 of the applicable school year.
5
6 (e) Each applicant district shall report to the
7 department on the expenditure of amounts awarded under this
8 section and shall provide other information as required by
9 rule of the department. Amounts awarded under this section
10 shall only be expended on supplies, materials and equipment
11 for career and technical education programs.
12
13 (f) The department of education shall promulgate
14 rules necessary to carry out this section.
15
16 (g) As used in this section, "career and technical
17 education program" shall be as specified in W.S.
18 21-13-309(m)(v)(D)(II).
19
