Severance tax distribution-highway fund.
Sponsored by: Joint Transportation, Highways & Military
Affairs Interim Committee
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to taxation and revenue; changing the
2 distribution of severance tax to the highway fund;
3 repealing a provision allowing for the distribution of
4 severance tax in the general appropriations bill; and
5 providing for an effective date.
6
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
8
9 Section 1. W.S. 39-14-801(b)(v) and by creating a new
10 paragraph (vii) is amended to read:
11
12 39-14-801. Severance tax distributions; distribution
13 account created; formula.
14
1 HB0029
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0258
1 (b) Before making distributions from the severance
2 tax distribution account under subsections (c) through (e)
3 of this section, an amount equal to two-thirds (2/3) of the
4 amount of tax collected under W.S. 39-14-104(a)(i) and
5 (b)(i) and 39-14-204(a)(i) for the same period shall be
6 deposited as follows:
7
8 (v) For fiscal year 2023 through fiscal year
9 2028 2025, these funds shall be deposited equally to the
10 permanent Wyoming mineral trust fund and to the common
11 school account within the permanent land fund;
12
13 (vii) For fiscal year 2026 and thereafter, these
14 funds shall be deposited to the highway fund.
15
16 Section 2. W.S. 39-14-801(b)(vi) and (f) is repealed.
17
18 Section 3. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
