4 "[COMPETITIVE GRANT PROGRAM FOR K-12 SCHOOL RESOURCE OFFICERS]
5
6 Section 351.
7
8 (a) There is appropriated five million dollars
9 ($5,000,000.00) from the public school foundation program account
10 to the education-school finance, section 2, section 205 of this
11 act, to establish a school district competitive grant program to
12 reimburse each district for the expense to employ not more than
13 one (1) school resource officer for school year 2025-2026 in the
14 district. Amounts reimbursed under this section shall be in
15 addition to and shall not be considered in determining the
16 district's foundation program amount under the education resource
17 block grant model pursuant to W.S. 21-13-309. Any reimbursement
18 under this section shall not exceed the amount expended by the
19 district to employ one (1) school resource officer. If the number
20 of applications exceed the funds appropriated under this section,
21 the districts' awarded funds shall be selected on a first-come,
22 first-served basis.
23
24 (b) The state superintendent of public instruction shall
25 promulgate rules necessary to carry out this section.
26
27 (c) It is the intent of the legislature that the appropriation
28 in this section not be included in the education-school finance's
29 standard budget for the immediately succeeding fiscal biennium.".
30
31 To the extent required by this amendment: adjust totals; and
32 renumber as necessary. COOPER
