5 Section 351.
6
7 (a) Of the full-time positions for the executive branch
8 authorized in this act that are unfilled on the effective date of
9 this act and of executive branch vacant positions as of June 30,
10 2025, the governor shall identify twenty-four (24) full-time
11 positions that, if eliminated or unfilled, would generate not less
12 than two million one hundred eight thousand one hundred twelve
13 dollars ($2,108,112.00) of annual savings. In determining the
14 savings for each position, the savings shall equal the annualized
15 appropriation for the position but in no event less than eighty-
16 seven thousand eight hundred thirty-eight dollars ($87,838.00) per
17 position. Authorization for each of the identified full-time
18 positions shall be eliminated and the amount of savings calculated
19 for each position shall be transferred pursuant to subsection (c)
20 of this section.
21
22 (b) Not later than October 1, 2025, the governor shall report
23 to the joint appropriations committee on the positions identified
24 in subsection (a) of this section as positions for which associated
25 general funds and other funds will be deposited in accordance with
26 subsection (c) of this section.
27
28 (c) Not later than November 1, 2025, all savings for each
29 eliminated position attributable to the general fund shall be
30 deposited by the state auditor in the general fund. All savings
31 from the eliminated positions attributable to other funds shall
32 remain in the account from which the funds would have been expended
33 for the position until further authorization by the legislature.
34 The authority to hire the full-time positions identified by the
35 governor under subsection (a) of this section shall terminate on
36 October 1, 2025.".
