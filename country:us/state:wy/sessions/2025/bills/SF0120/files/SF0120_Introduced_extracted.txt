SENATE FILE NO. SF0120
Wyoming PRIME act.
Sponsored by: Senator(s) Salazar and Pearson and
Representative(s) Andrew, Banks, Brady,
Brown, G, Hoeft, Lien, McCann, Neiman,
Schmid, Styvar, Webb, Webber and Wharff
for
1 AN ACT relating to marketing homemade foods; providing for
2 the sale of producer-raised, homemade meat products as
3 specified; providing a delayed effective date pending
4 certification by the governor based on federal action;
5 providing definitions; authorizing rulemaking; and
6 providing for an effective date.
7
8 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
9
10 Section 1. W.S. 11-49-102(a) by creating a new
11 paragraph (xiii) and by renumbering (xiii) as (xiv) and
12 11-49-103(c)(v) by creating a new subparagraph (G) and by
13 creating new subsections (n) and (o) are amended to read:
14

1 11-49-102. Definitions.
2
3 (a) As used in this act:
4
5 (xiii) "Custom slaughter facility" means a
6 slaughter facility that does not have a state or federal
7 inspector on duty and where any meat produced from the
8 facility is not considered to be state or federally
9 inspected meat products;
10
11 (xiii)(xiv) "This act" means W.S. 11-49-101
12 through 11-49-104.
13
14 11-49-103. Wyoming Food Freedom Act; purpose;
15 exemptions; assumption of risk.

18
19 (v) Not involve the sale of meat products, with
20 the following exceptions:

2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0444
1 (G) The sale of meat products under
2 subsection (n) of this section, subject to subsection (o)
3 of this section.
4
5 (n) Except as prohibited by federal law and subject
6 to subsection (o) of this section, a producer may sell meat
7 products from cattle, sheep, swine or goats raised by the
8 producer that were slaughtered on the premises of the
9 producer or at a custom slaughter facility, if all of the
10 following conditions are met:
11
12 (i) The meat products are produced from animals
13 that are raised, slaughtered, processed and sold in
14 Wyoming;
15
16 (ii) The meat products are sold directly to an
17 informed end consumer in Wyoming, whether for consumption
18 on or off the premises of the producer;
19
20 (iii) A prominent written warning statement is
21 delivered to the informed end consumer at the time of sale,
22 or the warning is displayed on a label affixed to the meat
3 SF0120
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0444
1 product packaging. The warning required by this paragraph
2 shall state:
3
4 (A) The meat product has not been inspected
5 and is not regulated;
6
7 (B) An informed end consumer who obtains
8 meat products under this act agrees not to sell, donate or
9 commercially redistribute the meat;
10
11 (C) Information describing the standards
12 used by the producer with respect to animal health and in
13 the processing of meat from the animal.
14
15 (iv) The producer shall not publish any
16 statement associated with the meat that implies or states
17 the Wyoming department of agriculture's approval or
18 endorsement of meat delivered under this subsection.
19
20 (o) Subsection (n) of this section shall be effective
21 on the date that the governor, on advice of the attorney
22 general, certifies to the secretary of state that the sale
23 of meat products from cattle, sheep, swine or goats raised
4 SF0120
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0444
1 by the producer that were slaughtered on the premises of
2 the producer or at a custom slaughter facility is legalized
3 under federal law. Legalization under federal law shall
4 constitute either the passage of a federal law that allows
5 direct-to-consumer sales of uninspected meat products or
6 the issuance of a final decision by a federal court with
7 jurisdiction over this state that declares that the federal
8 prohibition on direct-to-consumer sales is unconstitutional
9 or otherwise invalid. Sales of meat products under
10 subsection (n) of this section shall be allowed upon the
11 governor's certification to the secretary of state that
12 sales of uninspected meat products have been legalized
13 under federal law.
14
15 Section 2.
16
17 (a) Upon certification by the governor that W.S.
18 11-49-103(n), as created by section 1 of this act, is
19 effective:
20
21 (i) The department of agriculture may promulgate
22 any rules necessary to implement this act;
23

2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0444
1 (ii) Producers may sell meat products
2 immediately upon certification in accordance with W.S.
3 11-49-103(n) and (o), as created by section 1 of this act.
4
5 Section 3. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
