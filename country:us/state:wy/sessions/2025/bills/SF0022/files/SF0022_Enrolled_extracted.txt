Section 1. W.S. 14-2-309(a) by creating a new
paragraph (x) is amended to read:
14-2-309. Grounds for termination of parent-child
relationship; clear and convincing evidence.
(a) The parent-child legal relationship may be
terminated if any one (1) or more of the following facts is
established by clear and convincing evidence:
(x) The child has been appointed a guardian who
is a relative of the child, the child has been under the
guardian's care for at least twenty-four (24) consecutive
months and a showing that the parent is unfit to have
custody and control of the child. As used in this
paragraph, "relative" means as defined by W.S.
3-2-301(a)(iii).
