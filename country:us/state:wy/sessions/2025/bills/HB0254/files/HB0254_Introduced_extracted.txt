A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to legislative process; requiring the
2 installation of microphones at the desks of members in the
3 house of representatives; providing an appropriation; and
4 providing for an effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1.
9
10 (a) There is appropriated ninety-three thousand
11 dollars ($93,000.00) from the general fund to the
12 legislative service office. These funds shall be used only
13 for the purpose of the planning, design, purchase and
14 installation of microphones at each legislator's desk in
15 the chambers of the house of representatives. The
1 HB0254
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0721
1 microphones installed under this subsection are intended to
2 be used to enable the roll call votes of each legislator to
3 be heard within the chambers and during any broadcast of
4 the legislative proceedings.
5
6 (b) The funds appropriated in subsection (a) of this
7 section shall not be transferred or expended for any
8 purpose other than for the planning, design, purchase and
9 installation of microphones as required by subsection (a)
10 of this section. Any unexpended, unobligated amount
11 remaining from funds appropriated under this act shall
12 revert on June 30, 2026.
13
14 Section 2. This act is effective immediately upon
15 completion of all acts necessary for a bill to become law
16 as provided by Article 4, Section 8 of the Wyoming
