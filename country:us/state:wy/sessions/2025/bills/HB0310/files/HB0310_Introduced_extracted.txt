Trailer permanent registration-amendments.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Tarver, Banks, Campbell, K,
Davis, Haroldson, Knapp, Schmid, Wharff and
Wylie and Senator(s) Crago and Ide
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to motor vehicles; amending eligibility
2 requirements for permanent registrations of a trailer;
3 providing for the permanent registration of trailers that
4 qualify as commercial vehicles as specified; providing a
5 fee exemption for permanently registered trailers as
6 specified; making conforming amendments; specifying
7 applicability; and providing for an effective date.
8

13 subsections (aa) through (dd) are amended to read:

1 (m) Fifty percent (50%) of the permanent registration
2 fee collected under W.S. 31-2-206(n) or 31-18-201(aa) shall
3 be distributed by county treasurers in the same proportion
4 and manner as property taxes are distributed. The remaining
5 fifty percent (50%) of the permanent registration fee
6 collected under W.S. 31-2-206(n) or 31-18-201(aa) shall be
7 distributed to the highway fund.
8
9 31-18-201. Commercial vehicles; registration;
10 exemptions; permanent registration of trailers.
11
12 (aa) Except for a trailer that is mobile equipment or
13 a power unit, the owner of a trailer as defined in W.S.
14 31-1-101(a)(xxiii) that qualifies as part of a commercial
15 vehicle as defined in W.S. 31-1-101(a)(i) and is otherwise
16 subject to W.S. 31-18-401 may instead permanently register
17 the trailer upon payment of a fee in the amount of three
18 hundred fifty dollars ($350.00) at the time of initial
19 registration. Alternatively, the owner may permanently
20 register the trailer by paying the fees under subsection
21 (bb) of this section.

1 (bb) Upon payment of the fees specified in subsection
2 (aa) of this section or this subsection, the department
3 shall issue a distinctive license plate indicating the
4 permanent registration of the trailer. After payment of the
5 fees under subsection (aa) of this section or this
6 subsection, the owner of a trailer that is permanently
7 registered under subsection (aa) of this section shall not
8 be subject to additional registration fees for as long as
9 the owner owns the trailer. If the one-time fee specified
10 in subsection (aa) of this section is not paid, the fees
11 for permanent registration of a trailer under subsection
12 (aa) of this section are:

18 (ii) An amount equal to five (5) times the
19 applicable fees under W.S. 31-3-101(a), plus any donation
20 amounts under W.S. 31-3-101(h) or (j). Any donated amount
21 shall not be subject to the requirement to pay five (5)
22 times the applicable amount.

2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0404
1 (cc) The owner of a trailer that is permanently
2 registered under subsection (aa) of this section shall
3 display the permanent registration license plate in
4 accordance with W.S. 31-2-205 only on the permanently
5 registered trailer for which the plate was issued, except
6 when transferred pursuant to W.S. 31-2-214(e).
7
8 (dd) The permanent registration of a trailer under
9 subsection (aa) of this section shall not be transferred to
10 a new owner. Upon transfer of a trailer that had been
11 permanently registered under subsection (aa) of this
12 section to a new owner, the new owner shall be subject to
13 all applicable title, registration and license fees under
14 title 31, chapter 2, articles 1 and 2, chapter 3 and
15 chapter 18, articles 2 and 4 of the Wyoming statutes as
16 applicable for the trailer. The new owner may permanently
17 register the trailer as provided in this section.
18
19 Section 2. W.S. 31-2-206(n)(i) is repealed.
20
21 Section 3. This act shall apply to trailers
22 registered on and after the effective date of this act.
