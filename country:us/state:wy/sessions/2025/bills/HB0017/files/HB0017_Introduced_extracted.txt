Career technical education equipment grants amendments.
Sponsored by: Joint Education Interim Committee
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to career technical education equipment
2 grants for K-12 public school districts; increasing the
3 maximum grant award; reducing the years required to expend
4 funds for grant eligibility; and providing for an effective
5 date.
6
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
8
9 Section 1. W.S. 21-13-337(b)(intro) and (c) is
10 amended to read:
11
12 21-13-337. Career and technical education equipment
13 grants; application; criteria; limitations.
14
15 (b) A grant awarded under this section shall be for a
16 period of two (2) years and shall not exceed fifty thousand
