2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0756
SENATE FILE NO. SF0171
City and Town Regulatory Reduction.
Sponsored by: Senator(s) Nethercott and Cooper and
Representative(s) Byron, Lawley and Wylie
for
1 AN ACT relating to cities and towns; amending requirements
2 for conducting a boundary survey of cities and towns as
3 specified; and providing for an effective date.
4
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
6
7 Section 1. W.S. 15-1-413(a) is amended to read:
8
9 15-1-413. Survey of boundaries; when and how to be
10 made; presumption once recorded.
11
12 (a) If the boundaries of any city or town are
13 uncertain or incapable of ascertainment, and upon the
14 change in boundaries of any city or town pursuant to any
15 annexation under this chapter, the governing body, by
1 SF0171
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0756
1 ordinance, shall may provide for a survey thereof. When If
2 the survey is made, the boundaries shall be marked by
3 substantial monuments, and the person making the survey
4 shall report to the governing body describing the
5 boundaries by metes and bounds. The description as nearly
6 as possible shall refer, if upon surveyed lands, to the
7 corners or lines of the United States surveys. The person
8 making the survey shall also file with the city or town
9 clerk the field notes of his survey. The city or town
10 clerk shall then file a copy of the report and a copy of
11 the field notes certified by the mayor and clerk with the
12 county clerk for the county in which the city or town is
13 located.
14
15 Section 2. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
16
17 (END)
