Boards and commissions veteran ex officio members.
Affairs Interim Committee
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to boards and commissions; adding ex
2 officio nonvoting members who represent veterans and
3 veteran services to the University of Wyoming board of
4 trustees and the Wyoming community college commission as
5 specified; amending the duties of the Wyoming veterans'
6 commission; and providing for an effective date.
7
8 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
9
10 Section 1. W.S. 19-14-107(c) by creating a new
11 paragraph (xiii), 21-17-207 and 21-18-201(c) are amended to
12 read:
13
14 19-14-107. Creation of veterans' commission;
15 composition; terms.
1 SF0031
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0255
1
2 (c) The commission shall:
3
4 (xiii) Recommend members of the public
5 representing veterans and veteran services to be appointed
6 by the governor to the university board of trustees and the
7 Wyoming community college commission to serve as ex officio
8 nonvoting members.
9
10 21-17-207. University board composition; ex officio
11 members.
12
13 (a) In addition to the members of the university
14 board of trustees specified under W.S. 21-17-201, the
15 director of the Wyoming community college commission
16 following members shall serve as a member members ex
17 officio, having the right to speak but not to vote:.
18
19 (i) The director of the Wyoming community
20 college commission;
21
2 SF0031
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0255
1 (ii) A member of the public representing
2 veterans and veteran services recommended by the Wyoming
3 veterans' commission and appointed by the governor.
4
5 21-18-201. Community college commission; created;
6 composition; removal.
7
8 (c) The governor, and the state superintendent of
9 public instruction and a member of the public representing
10 veterans and veteran services recommended by the Wyoming
11 veterans' commission and appointed by the governor are ex
12 officio nonvoting members of the commission.
13
14 Section 2. This act is effective immediately upon
