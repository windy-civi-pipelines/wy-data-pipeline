AN ACT relating to K-12 school facilities; repealing the
requirement for charter schools to receive reimbursement
for leasing expenses if the state construction department
determines there are no existing adequate educational
facilities within the school district; making conforming
amendments; and providing for an effective date.
Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
Section 1. W.S. 21-15-123(f)(ix) is amended to read:
21-15-123. State construction department; duties and
authority relating to school facilities.
(f) The state construction department shall:
(ix) Review requests submitted by charter
schools pursuant to W.S. 21-3-110(a)(x) and 21-3-304(k) to
determine whether space exists within the applicable
district for operation of the charter school that is
available and adequate.
Section 2. W.S. 21-3-110(a)(x)(A)(II) is repealed.
