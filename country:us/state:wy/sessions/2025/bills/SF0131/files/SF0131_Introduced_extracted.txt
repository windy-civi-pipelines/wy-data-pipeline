2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0654
SENATE FILE NO. SF0131
Charter school leasing.
Hutchings, Olsen and Salazar and
Representative(s) Bear and Neiman
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to relating to K-12 school facilities;
2 repealing the requirement that charter schools receive
3 reimbursement for leasing expenses if the state
4 construction department determines there are no existing
5 adequate educational facilities within the school district;
6 making conforming amendments; and providing for an
7 effective date.
8
9 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
10
11 Section 1. W.S. 21-15-123(f)(ix) is amended to read:
12
13 21-15-123. State construction department; duties and
14 authority relating to school facilities.
15
1 SF0131
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0654
1 (f) The state construction department shall:
2
3 (ix) Review requests submitted by charter
4 schools pursuant to W.S. 21-3-110(a)(x) and 21-3-304(k) to
5 determine whether space exists within the applicable
6 district for operation of the charter school that is
7 available and adequate.
8
9 Section 2. W.S. 21-3-110(a)(x)(A)(II) is repealed.
10
11 Section 3. This act is effective immediately upon
12 completion of all acts necessary for a bill to become law
13 as provided by Article 4, Section 8 of the Wyoming
14 Constitution.
