2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0577
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION NO. SJ0004
Sponsored by: Senator(s) Pearson, Smith, D and Steinmetz and
Representative(s) Andrew, Angelos, Brady,
Guggenmos, Lien, Lucas, McCann, Webber and
Wharff
A JOINT RESOLUTION
for
1 A JOINT RESOLUTION requesting the Governor concentrate on
2 promoting stable dispatchable base load power resources from
3 Wyoming in lieu of taxpayer subsidized commercial wind and
4 solar generated power.
5
6 WHEREAS, Wyoming produces four (4) of the cleanest resources
7 of stable dispatchable base load power generation in the
8 United States: clean coal, natural gas, uranium and
9 hydroelectric;
10
11 WHEREAS, Wyoming has the ability to provide these resources
12 to the rest of the United States and the world for stable
13 dispatchable base load power generation.
14
1
SJ0004
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0577
1 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE MEMBERS OF THE
2 LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF WYOMING:
3
4 Section 1. That the Legislature of the State of Wyoming
5 respectfully requests the Governor to oppose future promotion
6 and subsidization of commercial wind and solar power
7 generation in the state of Wyoming and promote efforts that
8 provide Wyoming's stable dispatchable base load energy to all
9 of Wyoming's citizens. The Legislature further requests the
10 Governor to promote the export of Wyoming's stable
11 dispatchable base load energy resources to the rest of the
12 United States and the world.
13
14 Section 2. That the Secretary of State of Wyoming
15 transmit a copy of this resolution to the Governor.
16
17 (END)
2
SJ0004
