ORIGINAL SENATE
JOINT RESOLUTION. SJ0004
ENROLLED JOINT RESOLUTION NO. 2, SENATE
SIXTY-EIGHTH LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF WYOMING
2025 GENERAL SESSION
A JOINT RESOLUTION requesting the Governor concentrate on
promoting stable dispatchable base load power resources from
Wyoming in lieu of taxpayer subsidized commercial wind and
solar generated power.
WHEREAS, Wyoming produces four (4) of the cleanest resources
of stable dispatchable base load power generation in the
United States: clean coal, natural gas, uranium and
hydroelectric;
WHEREAS, Wyoming has the ability to provide these resources
to the rest of the United States and the world for stable
dispatchable base load power generation.
NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED BY THE MEMBERS OF THE
LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF WYOMING:
Section 1. That the Legislature of the State of Wyoming
respectfully requests the Governor to oppose future promotion
and subsidization of commercial wind and solar power
generation in the state of Wyoming and promote efforts that
provide Wyoming's stable dispatchable base load energy to all
of Wyoming's citizens. The Legislature further requests the
Governor to promote the export of Wyoming's stable
dispatchable base load energy resources to the rest of the
United States and the world.
