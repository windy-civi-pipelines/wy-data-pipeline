1 AN ACT relating to homeland security; amending duties of

3 the director of the office of homeland security for
4 identification of conveyances near critical infrastructure;

6 critical infrastructure zones to the county clerks;
7 amending and creating definitions; and providing for an
8 effective date.

20
21 (a) As used in this article:

1 (i) "Conveyance" means as defined by W.S.
2 34-1-102 and includes conveyances of every instrument in
3 writing by which any interest in real property, surface
4 interests, mineral interests and pore space interests is
5 created, alienated, mortgaged, leased or assigned, or by

17 and appurtenances, including structures, affixed thereto,
18 and any intangible characteristic that contributes to the
19 fair market value thereof. "Real property" includes surface
20 interests, mineral interests and pore space interests.

2 (c) Within fifteen (15) days after the a conveyance
3 is executed and completed recorded, the county clerk shall
4 report each the conveyance that involves any property

6 located not more than five (5) miles from a critical

15 infrastructure zone and, upon reasonable suspicion,
16 investigate the conveyance to determine if the conveyance
17 involves a designated country or person or if the
18 conveyance poses a threat to national or state security or
19 to critical infrastructure.
