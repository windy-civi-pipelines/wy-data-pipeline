5 "(i) "Abortion" means the act of using or prescribing
6 any instrument, medicine, drug or any other substance, device or
7 means with the intent to terminate the clinically diagnosable
8 pregnancy of a woman, including the elimination of one (1) or more
9 unborn babies in a multifetal pregnancy, with knowledge that the

11 the death of the unborn baby. "Abortion" shall not include any
12 use, prescription or means specified in this paragraph if the use,

17
18 (B) Remove a dead unborn baby caused by
19 spontaneous abortion or intrauterine fetal demise;
20
21 (C) Treat a woman for an ectopic pregnancy; or
22
23 (D) Treat a woman for cancer or another disease
24 that requires medical treatment which treatment may be fatal or
25 harmful to the unborn baby.". STEINMETZ
