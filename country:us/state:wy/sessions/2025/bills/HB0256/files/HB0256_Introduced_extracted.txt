5 election, a select committee on blockchain, financial

7 technologies shall be appointed subject to the following:

23 continued leadership in the digital economy by:

2 (A) Reducing impediments to economic

8
9 (C) Promoting further development of
10 digital commercial activity in the state.
11

13 election, the governor shall appoint three (3) persons to
