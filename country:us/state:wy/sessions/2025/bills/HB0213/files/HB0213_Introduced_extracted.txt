Sponsored by: Representative(s) Chestek, Filer, Harshman

A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to residential rental property; removing
2 the exception for parties to agree in writing that the
3 rental unit is not required to have operational utilities;
4 and providing for an effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 1-21-1202(a) is amended to read:
9
10 1-21-1202. Duties of owners and renters; generally.
11
12 (a) Each owner and his agent renting or leasing a
13 residential rental unit shall maintain that unit in a safe
14 and sanitary condition fit for human habitation. Each
15 residential rental unit shall have operational electrical,

1 heating and plumbing, with hot and cold running water,
2 unless otherwise agreed upon in writing by both parties.
3 provided, however, this section shall not prevent the
4 rental of seasonal rental units such as summer cabins which
5 are not intended to have such amenities.
6
7 Section 2. This act is effective July 1, 2025.

9 (END)
