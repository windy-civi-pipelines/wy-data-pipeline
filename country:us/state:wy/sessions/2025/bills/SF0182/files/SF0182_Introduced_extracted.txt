Property tax valuation-fair market value.
Erickson
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to property taxation; revising the
2 definition of fair market value to include replacement cost
3 new less depreciation for specified property; making
4 conforming amendments; requiring rulemaking; and providing
5 for effective dates.
6
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
8
9 Section 1. W.S. 39-11-101(a)(vi) and 39-11-102(c)(xv)
10 are amended to read:
11
12 39-11-101. Definitions.
13
14 (a) As used in this act unless otherwise specifically
15 provided:

1
2 (vi) "Fair market value" means:
3
4 (A) For land, the amount in cash, or terms
5 reasonably equivalent to cash, a well informed buyer is
6 justified in paying for a property and a well informed
7 seller is justified in accepting, assuming neither party to
8 the transaction is acting under undue compulsion, and
9 assuming the property has been offered in the open market
10 for a reasonable time, except, fair market value of
11 agricultural land shall be determined as provided by W.S.
12 39-13-103(b)(x); and fair market value of
13
14 (B) For personal property and for
15 appurtenances, including structures, affixed to land, the
16 replacement cost new of the property, less depreciation;
17
18 (C) For mine products, fair market value
19 shall be determined as provided by W.S. 39-14-103(b),
20 39-14-203(b), 39-14-303(b), 39-14-403(b), 39-14-503(b),
21 39-14-603(b) and 39-14-703(b).;
22

2 department of revenue.
3
4 (c) In addition to the other powers and duties
5 imposed by law, the department shall:
6
7 (xv) Prescribe the system of establishing
8 manuals, formulas, methods or systems required to establish
9 the fair market value of all property valued for property
10 taxation to ensure that all property within a class is
11 uniformly valued. The county assessor and the facilities of
12 his office, together with the deputy assessors and clerical
13 assistants in each county, at the direction of the
14 department, shall give full aid in the installation of the
15 any prescribed system in the county. The county shall also
16 furnish the necessary supplies and records for installing
17 the any system under this paragraph;
18
19 Section 2. The department of revenue shall adopt
20 rules necessary to implement this act not later than
