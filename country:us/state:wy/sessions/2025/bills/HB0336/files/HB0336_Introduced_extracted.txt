Waiver of parental rights-financial responsibility.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Webber, Andrew, Brady,

A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to children; requiring a written
2 acknowledgement in a voluntary termination of parental
3 rights as specified; and providing for an effective date.
4
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
6
7 Section 1. W.S. 14-2-320 is created to read:
8
9 14-2-320. Voluntary termination of parental rights;
10 written acknowledgement.
11
12 (a) Any voluntary termination of parental rights,
13 whether through a petition or agreement, shall include a
14 written acknowledgement from the parent who is voluntarily
15 terminating parental rights that the termination of
1 HB0336
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0579
1 parental rights may not terminate, suspend or otherwise
2 affect any existing or future child support obligations.
3
4 (b) The acknowledgement required under subsection (a)
5 of this section shall be signed by the parent who is
6 voluntarily terminating their parental rights and shall be
7 made part of the official record in any proceeding related
8 to the termination.
9
10 (c) Nothing in this section shall be construed to
11 limit the ability of a court to modify, enforce or
12 terminate child support obligations in accordance with
13 applicable law.
14
15 Section 2. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
