Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0730
SENATE FILE NO. SF0162
Education-block grant model amendments.
and Representative(s) Andrew and Schmid
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to school finance; restricting and amending
2 the expenditure of teacher resources and other funds
3 generated by the education resource block grant model as
4 specified; requiring data collection; requiring reporting;
5 requiring a study; and providing for an effective date.
6
7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
8
9 Section 1. W.S. 21-13-338 is created to read:
10
11 21-13-338. Expenditure of funds generated for
12 teachers.
13
14 (a) Beginning with school year 2025-2026 and for each
15 school year thereafter and except as provided in subsection
1 SF0162
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0730
1 (b) of this section, all funds allocated for teachers by
2 the education resource block grant model under Attachment
3 A(b)(iii) through (v), including resources generated for
4 salaries, health insurance and other benefits, shall be
5 separately accounted for by each school district and shall
6 be expended only for the purposes of teacher salaries and
7 benefits.
8
9 (b) Subsection (a) of this section shall not apply to
10 charter schools operating under chapter 3, article 3 of
11 this title, alternative schools operating under W.S.
12 21-13-309(m)(v)(B), schools with less than forty-nine (49)
13 students or school districts with less than two hundred
14 forty-three (243) students.
15
16 (c) Nothing in this section shall be construed to
17 prohibit a school district from allocating or expending
18 funds provided under the education resource block grant
19 model for teacher resources specified in subsection (a) of
20 this section. Except as provided in subsection (b) of this
21 section, school districts shall not expend teacher
22 resources specified in subsection (a) of this section for
2 SF0162
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0730
1 purposes other than those specified in subsection (a) of
2 this section.
3
4 (d) The department of education shall collect data
5 from school districts on the amounts expended on teacher
6 salaries, health insurance, other benefits and the number
7 of teachers employed by each school district in accordance
8 with W.S. 21-2-203. Not later than October 1 of each year,
9 the department shall report to the joint education interim
10 committee on the data collected under this subsection.
11
12 Section 2.
13
14 (a) Not later than October 1, 2025 and again on
15 October 1, 2026, the department of education shall report
16 to the joint education interim committee and any committee
17 established for the recalibration of the education resource
18 block grant model under W.S. 21-13-309(t) on findings and
19 recommendations on data collected under W.S. 21-13-338(d),
20 as created by section 1 of this act. The report required
21 under this subsection shall include any recommendations for
22 refinement of components within the education resource
3 SF0162
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0730
1 block grant model allocated for teacher salaries, health
2 insurance and other benefits.
3
4 (b) Not later than June 30, 2026, the data advisory
5 committee established under W.S. 21-2-203(d) shall provide
6 recommendations to the state superintendent of public
7 instruction to clearly identify expenditures on teacher
8 salaries, health insurance and other benefits by school
9 districts for purposes of implementing W.S. 21-13-338, as
10 created by section 1 of this act.
11
12 Section 3. This act is effective immediately upon
13 completion of all acts necessary for a bill to become law
14 as provided by Article 4, Section 8 of the Wyoming
15 Constitution.
16
17 (END)
