2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0792
Instream flow amendments.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Storer
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to water; allowing for the private
2 ownership of water rights for instream flow purposes; and
3 providing for an effective date.
4
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
6
7 Section 1. W.S. 41-3-1002(e) and 41-3-1007 by
8 creating a new subsection (b) and by renumbering (b) as (c)
9 are amended to read:
10
11 41-3-1002. Instream flows to be by stream segment;
12 waters for instream flows may be sold, transferred or
13 otherwise conveyed under certain restrictions; ownership
14 restricted.
15
1 HB0334
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0792
1 (e) No person other than The state of Wyoming shall
2 or any person may own any instream flow water right.
3
4 41-3-1007. Acquisition of existing rights for
5 instream flow purposes.
6
7 (b) Any person may acquire an existing water right or
8 petition for a change of use for instream flow purposes in
9 accordance with this chapter.
10
11 (b)(c) Any such water rights acquired and changed
12 shall be limited to a specified stream segment by the board
13 of control with priority date intact.
14
15 Section 2. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
