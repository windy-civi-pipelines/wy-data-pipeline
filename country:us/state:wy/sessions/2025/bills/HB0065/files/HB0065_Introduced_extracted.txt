Executive orders repository.
Sponsored by: Joint Corporations, Elections & Political
Subdivisions Interim Committee
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to the administration of the government;
2 providing a process for publishing and maintaining
3 executive orders; providing a definition; requiring
4 previously issued executive orders be published and
5 maintained on the secretary of state website; and providing
6 for an effective date.
7
8 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
9
10 Section 1. W.S. 9-1-302(a)(ii) and 9-1-303(d) by
11 creating a new paragraph (iii) are amended to read:
12
13 9-1-302. Powers and duties; custodian of public
14 records; preservation of executive orders and proclamations
1 HB0065
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0009
1 of governor; removal of public documents prohibited;
2 exceptions.
3
4 (a) The secretary of state is the custodian of, and
5 shall preserve:
6
7 (ii) All executive orders issued by a governor,
8 proclamations of the governor proclaiming the effectiveness
9 of constitutional amendments, corporate revocations and any
10 other proclamation designated as a permanent record by the
11 governor. The secretary of state shall preserve all other
12 proclamations of the governor for one (1) year and then
13 deliver them to the department of state parks and cultural
14 resources for destruction or preservation as it deems
15 desirable. As used in this paragraph, "executive order"
16 means a proclamation or order executed by the governor
17 pursuant to constitutional or statutory authority to
18 implement any provision of law; provided "executive order"
19 does not include:
20
21 (A) A proclamation issued by the governor
22 calling a special session of the legislature;
23

6 W.S. 22-20-201;
7
8 (D) A proclamation of the adoption of a
9 constitutional amendment.
10
11 9-1-303. Powers and duties; affixing seal to and
12 countersigning commissions and documents; certified copies
13 of acts; file of commissions and appointments; publication
14 of documents.
15
16 (d) The secretary of state shall make the following
17 documents available to the public by publishing the
18 documents on the secretary of state's official website:
19
20 (iii) All executive orders, as defined in W.S.
21 9-1-302(a)(ii), issued by a governor.
22

1 Section 2. Any executive order previously issued by a
2 Wyoming governor prior to the effective date of this act
3 that can be reasonably located shall be published on the
4 secretary of state's official website as soon as reasonably
5 practicable in accordance with this act. The requirements
6 of this section shall include executive orders that have
7 expired.
8
9 Section 3. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
