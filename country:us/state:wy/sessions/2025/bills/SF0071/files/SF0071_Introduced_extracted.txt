7 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
8

10
11 6-2-106. Homicide by vehicle; aggravated homicide by
12 vehicle; penalties.
13
14 (d) Any person convicted of aggravated homicide by
15 vehicle for causing the death of another person while
1 SF0071

1 operating or driving a vehicle in violation of W.S.
2 31-5-233 shall not be issued an ignition interlock
3 restricted license under W.S. 31-5-233 or 31-7-401 through

5 of revocation provided under W.S. 31-7-127(b) for each
6 conviction under this section.
7
8 Section 2. This act shall apply to persons convicted
9 of aggravated homicide by vehicle for causing the death of
10 another person while operating or driving a vehicle in
11 violation of W.S. 31-5-233 before, on and after July 1,

13
14 Section 3. This act is effective July 1, 2025.
