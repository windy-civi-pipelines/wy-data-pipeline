Working animal protection act.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Rodriguez-Williams,
Allemand, Webber and Wharff
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to counties, cities and towns; prohibiting
2 counties, cities and towns from enacting and enforcing
3 policies on the use of animals as specified; and providing
4 for an effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 15-1-133 and 18-2-117 are created to
9 read:
10
11 15-1-133. Working animal protections.
12
13 (a) No city or town shall enact an ordinance or
14 policy that terminates, bans or unduly restricts a person

5 or policy that conflicts with this section, but shall not
6 supersede:
7
8 (i) Any municipal zoning ordinance enacted under
9 this title;
10
11 (ii) Any municipal ordinance or policy relating
12 to public health or public safety.
13
14 (c) This section shall not be interpreted to amend
15 any other Wyoming law that relates to animal care, public
16 health or public safety, including the provisions of W.S.
17 23-3-109.
