Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0260
Health mandates-CDC and WHO jurisdiction in Wyoming.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Lucas, Angelos, Brown, G,
Hoeft, Johnson, Kelly, McCann, Styvar,
Wasserburger, Webber and Wharff and
Senator(s) Pearson
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to public health and safety; prohibiting
2 health mandates and requirements for COVID-19 and any
3 variant thereof by specified federal and nongovernmental
4 health organizations; and providing for an effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 35-4-1101 is created to read:
9
10 ARTICLE 11
11 HEALTH ORGANIZATIONS RULES PROHIBITED
12
1 HB0141
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0260
1 35-4-1101. Consideration and compliance with
2 requirements of specified health organizations;
3 limitations.
4
5 The United States centers for disease control and
6 prevention and the world health organization shall have no
7 jurisdiction in Wyoming and any requirements, mandates,
8 recommendations, instructions or guidance provided,
9 promulgated or established by either organization shall not
10 be binding in this state to justify any mask, vaccine or
11 medical testing requirements for COVID-19 and any variant
12 thereof and shall have no force or effect in Wyoming.
13
14 Section 2. This act is effective immediately upon
15 completion of all acts necessary for a bill to become law
16 as provided by Article 4, Section 8 of the Wyoming
17 Constitution.
18
19 (END)
2 HB0141
