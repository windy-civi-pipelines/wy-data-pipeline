Section 1:
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0629
Bond election language process.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Lien, Campbell, K and Hoeft
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to elections; requiring review and approval
2 of bond questions by the secretary of state; amending
3 requirements for the bond question; and providing for an
4 effective date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 22-21-103 is amended to read:
9
10 22-21-103. How bond question to be submitted to
11 electors; contents; approval.
12
13 Each bond question shall be submitted to a vote of the
14 qualified electors of the political subdivision. Every bond
15 election shall be held on the same day as a primary
16 election or a general election, or on the Tuesday next
1 HB0190
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0629
1 following the first Monday in May or November, or on the
2 Tuesday next following the third Monday in August. Not less
3 than one hundred thirty (130) days before a bond election
4 and before submitting the bond question to the county clerk
5 under this section, the political subdivision shall submit
6 the proposed bond question to the secretary of state for
7 approval. Not less than one hundred twenty (120) days
8 before a bond election, the secretary of state shall
9 approve the form of the bond question if it complies with
10 this section, modify the language or form to conform with
11 this section if necessary and notify the political
12 subdivision of the approval or modifications of the bond
13 question. Not less than one hundred ten (110) days before a
14 bond election, the political subdivision shall provide
15 written notification to the county clerk specifying the
16 date of the election and the bond question. The bond
17 question shall state the purpose of the bonds in a true and
18 impartial manner, the maximum principal amount thereof, the
19 maximum number of years allowed for the indebtedness and
20 the maximum rate of interest to be paid thereon. The
21 secretary of state may promulgate reasonable rules for
22 conducting bond elections where the election is not held at
23 the same time as the general or primary election.
