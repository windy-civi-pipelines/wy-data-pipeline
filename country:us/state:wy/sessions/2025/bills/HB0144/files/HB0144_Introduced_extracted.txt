County canvassing board.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Neiman, Bear, Guggenmos,
Haroldson, Heiner, Kelly and Riggins and
Senator(s) Brennan, Hicks and Pearson
A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to elections; specifying the membership of
2 the county canvassing board; making conforming changes; and
3 providing for an effective date.
4
5 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
6
7 Section 1. W.S. 22-4-101(d), 22-16-101 and
8 22-29-114(e)(intro) are amended to read:
9
10 22-4-101. Application; composition, election and
11 qualifications of county central committees; certificate of
12 election.
13
14 (d) No later than forty-five (45) days after the date
15 of the primary election, the county canvassing board or a
1 HB0144
2025 STATE OF WYOMING 25LSO-0429
1 canvassing board appointed by the county clerk consisting
2 of two (2) electors and the county clerk, shall provide to
3 each county chairman the certified election results for
4 precinct committeemen and committeewomen and a list of
5 persons who received write-in votes for precinct
6 committeemen or committeewomen.
7
8 22-16-101. County canvassing board; compensation.
9
10 The county canvassing board is the county clerk and two (2)
11 electors of different political parties resident in who are
12 residents of the county, appointed with one (1) elector
13 selected by the county clerk central committee of each of
14 the two (2) largest major political parties in the county.
15 If there are not two (2) major political parties in the
16 county, or if a county central committee fails to select an
17 elector for the county canvassing board under this section,
18 the county clerk may appoint any unfilled positions on the
19 county canvassing board, provided that the two (2) electors
20 shall be of different political parties. The two (2)
21 electors shall receive the same compensation as election
22 judges. All canvassing board members shall take an oath of
23 office.

16 district election not conducted by a county clerk shall be

20 district board. Any canvass shall be conducted in

22 1973, as amended. The canvass shall be conducted within
