Columbarium regulation.
Sponsored by: Representative(s) Sherwood, Angelos and Bear

A BILL
for
1 AN ACT relating to cemeteries and burials; amending
2 provisions related to the structural integrity and
3 esthetics of columbariums; and providing for an effective
4 date.
5
6 Be It Enacted by the Legislature of the State of Wyoming:
7
8 Section 1. W.S. 35-8-402(a)(iv) through (vi) is
9 amended to read:
10
11 35-8-402. Regulations and specifications generally;
12 application to certain columbariums.
13
14 (a) No person, firm, partnership, association,
15 company or corporation shall construct any community vault,

1 crypt, columbarium or mausoleum, wholly or partially above

3 or remains of any dead human being unless the same shall be
4 located within the confines of an established cemetery
5 containing not less than five (5) acres, which cemetery
6 shall have been in existence and operation for a period of
7 at least five (5) years immediately preceding the time of
8 the erection thereof, nor until plans and specifications
9 therefor shall be approved by the department of health.

17 limestone, concrete block masonry or any other material of

20 to be structurally sound and to withstand exterior
